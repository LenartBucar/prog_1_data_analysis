About This Content

This Extra Character Pack for 100% Orange Juice adds 2 all-new playable characters from developer Orange_Juice - Lone Rider and Merchant, both Stage 3 bosses from "Flying Red Barrel", as well as character and announcer voices for them!

Owners of the Railway Runners Pack unlock the field "Lonely Railway" for free in-game.

Railway Runners Pack includes

  • Lone Rider, the charismatic man from 'Flying Red Barrel' on an endless journey (Voice: Terashima Junta)
  • Merchant, the cunning salesman from 'Flying Red Barrel' who delivers what he wants, where he wants (Voice: Yamanaka Masahiro)
  • New, unique Hyper Cards for both characters to bring chaos to the battlefield with!
  • New music tracks play as Lone Rider and Merchant's themes
  • Free unlock of the "Lonely Railway" field.

The new characters are designed by Orange_Juice's original staff for the game's English Steam release, with character art by Hono and hyper card art by Junpyon.
