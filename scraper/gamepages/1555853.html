About This Content

The contents of this DLC are as follows:

- Multiplies Monster Gold Cap by 2
- Multiplies Gold Gain by 2
- 5500 Epic Coin
- Multiplies EXP Gain by 1.5


This DLC expands Monster Gold Cap :)
It stacks with other DLC.

Note:
- Epic Coin is a premium currency. You can purchase premium items in Epic Store with it. You can also earn Epic Coin through Daily Quest, Missions and so on in the game.
- It also increases EXP Gain from Metal Slimes.
- There is Monster Gold Cap in the game, which normally limits your gold gain up to 1000 per monster. You can increase Monster Gold Cap with a Slime Bank Upgrade in the game.
ex) If the original gold gain is 700 from a slime and monster gold cap is 1000, you will gain 1400 gold from a slime with 2000 monster gold cap after purchasing this DLC.
