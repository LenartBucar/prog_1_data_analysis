About This Content

“Victory is the only thing that matters”
- Ezekyle Abaddon

Get a head start in The Horus Heresy: Legions with this amazing Starter Bundle! It brings amazing value-for-money and exclusive items:

  • A full deck of 30 cards, ‘Justaerin Commander’, with Abaddon as warlord and a Legendary card! It is ready to play with it directly, or you can use it to round up your Sons of Horus collection and get a handful of versatile neutral cards.
  • An exclusive alternate art version of Ezekyle Abaddon, First Captain of the Sons of Horus and right hand of the Warmaster.
  • An exclusive matching card back, ‘Justaerin Warplate’, based on Abaddon’s iconic Terminator Armour. The perfect fit for your Sons of Horus deck!
  • 1000 coins to spend on crates and expand your card collection.
  • 5 tickets to take part in Events, in which you can reenact the most famous battles of the Horus Heresy lore and discover event-exclusive warlords.

Jump-start your progress in the fight to reach Terra. Grab yourself this amazing deal and get ready to crush your enemies in The Horus Heresy: Legions!
