Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“"My Island" has now achieved a grand and magnificent open world, with a complete game framework, a beautiful dynamic weather system and some basic content, but it need to fill in more interesting content, and improve the polishing of more details, we look forward to the player experience and feedback, each update gives players a new experience.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Expected to the state of earlyaccess for about 1-2 years”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to add new content as follows:
- Add more abundant land animals and marine animals
- New tools and weapons, Rich food and recipes
- Add storyline and new characters
- More beautiful natural environment design and detail construction”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“At present, we have realized a huge and magnificent open world of 256 square kilometers, which can be explored for dozens of hours. Players can sail, hunt, plant, cook, Dynamically changing ocean and beautiful dynamic sky, dozens of terrestrial Animals and marine life, players can build their own buildings, craft weapons and survival props, can grow a variety of trees, although the game now has a complete game framework, but still need to add more elements to enrich the content.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to increase the price of the game after the full version release or major update, because the new contents will be very rich in the future, and will get a completely different experience. Players who have already purchased the earlyaccess version will receive all future updates and complete version game for free.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will be looking forward to the feedback and suggestions from the players to help us improve the game and let the player get new experience.”

About This Game

My Island is a fresh, beautiful survival game with endless blue tropical ocean, which is quite different from most bloody and repressive survival games.
You will drift on the vast ocean and start a lonely journey. Planting crop and trees, craft weapons, building houses,hunting and cooking, creating your own island home and Eden in dreams.

A grand open world

We have realized a huge open world with 256 square kilometers of areas. It is not a random generated meaningless endless worlds. Each island has different landscapes. There are hundreds of beautiful and unique islands in the map, everything is waiting for you to explore.
Landscapes: magnificent grasslands, canyon cliffs, hills, coconut groves, tropical rain forests, frigid forests,and even there is a secret world waiting for you to discover. you can also plant trees and flowers to change environment. The scenery is colorful, and each frame can be used as a wallpaper.

Powerful character customization system

you can customize different faces you want (this is also ready for the multiplayer mode), you can choose the gender for your character, Adjust face,Height, leg length, choice of hairstyle,clothes, fat and thin, skin color, hair color, etc.

Planting system

You can grow 7 kinds of coconut trees,apple trees,bamboo,Watermelon, tomato, cucumber, strawberry,wheat, corn, carrot (more trees and crops in the future). When you have seed and water, you can watch it break out of the ground, grow dynamically into a big tree, and then if you need to cut it with an axe to get trees and fruits, create your own forest and flower sea, beautify your warm home.

Super Dynamic Weather System

Fantastic super dynamic sky, complete dynamic day and night circulatory system, more than a dozen different random weather changes, clear sky and clouds, dark clouds, heavy rain, colorful clouds, dreamy sunrise and sunset, amazing heavy rain and thrilling lightning thunder... You will experience something you've never felt before. The wind in the game is also dynamic. Trees and oceans will change in different states with the wind. From calm to stormy waves, it will interfere with your raft.

Free construction and smelting system

You can build shelters and build houses by cutting down the trees and leaves. the building system is simple,You can upgrade wooden walls to brick and stone walls, if you have enough resources, you can create different styles of buildings as you wish. villas or wooden houses depend on your design. You can also plant trees and flowers around the house to beautify your home environment. you can also craft some weapons and survival items, campfires, axes, water collectors, and more.

Gourmet cooking system

If you like cooking food, and make life more interesting, you can now make several kinds of barbecue foods, you can put the meat and seafood on the campfire and stone. Cooking and barbecue, making pure natural delicacies, they are completely simulated physics, you can drag and drop them anywhere, this is a more intuitive and simple way of operation, after you kill an animal You can also drag it away.

Swimming ,Sailing,Taming horses and riding

You can create rafts and boats that you can drive on the ocean with dynamic wind and buoyancy. Dynamically changing waves can affect your speed, and huge waves can even flip your boat. You can also swim to explore the underwater world, shuttle through the coral and fishes, kiss the gentle white sharks, build some buildings on the sea floor, and even plant trees on the sea floor.

Animal AI system and Animal specimen craft

At present, there are more than a dozen land animals and fishes in the game, including Seagull, eagle,saltwater crocodile, great white shark, brown bear, prairie wolf, wild boar, hare, deer, viper, various marine fishes, etc., and more marine animals and birds will be added in the future,You can make the animal into a specimen and put it at home as a collection. Hunting will be very interesting.You can't imagine dozens of cold giant saltwater crocodiles lurking in the coconut grove on the beautiful island.

You can find a group of wild horses in the vast grasslands, you can tame them to ride horses, and freely run in the grasslands and forests.

Future update direction

Although the current version is enough for you to explore for dozens of hours, many details need to be added, we will add more crops and tree species that can be planted, richer weapons blueprints,build part and furniture, more variety of food and recipes , will add to the story and new characters, pet and animal breeding systems, fishing,airship and more.
