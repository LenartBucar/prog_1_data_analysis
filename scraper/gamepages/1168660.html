About This Game











Barro 2020 is the sequel for Barro with 8 new challenging race tracks.

Game modes:
  • Arcade: You vs 3 opponents, do your best and finish the race as the winner.
  • Time trial: You against yourself, finish the laps as fastest as you can.
  • Multiplayer: Play the multiplayer (split screen) with a friend and have fun.

Cars:
  • 4 types of cars, choose the one that fits you better.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7 SP1+
    • Processor: SSE2 instruction set support
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Graphics card with DX10 (shader model 4.0) capabilities
    • Storage: 300 MB available space
