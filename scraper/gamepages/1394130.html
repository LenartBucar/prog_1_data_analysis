Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We at Baronnerie Games love the concept of Early Access. Gathering players as soon as possible and receiving their feedback on early iterations of the game is something that's really important to us, as we're making games for the players!
We want to make sure that we can make the best product possible, and that can only be done with the help of the community!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We intend to remain very flexible and respond to player feedback during the Early Access process, so we can't give an exact timeline, but we expect the Early Access phase to last somewhere between 6 and 12 months!”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The Early Access version contains the majority of the core features of the game. We will use this phase mostly to add a lot of content to the game and to use community feedback to iterate on the game's current features. This means more heroes, more cards, more maps.. more of everything!
The current plan for the full release is to have 8 heroes and 6 areas to explore.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access version contains the majority of the mechanics of the game. There are 2 areas to explore and 5 heroes to explore them with.
The meta-progression is also included: Players can level-up their heroes, unlock permanent upgrades by spending gold, progressively unlock more and more cards to build their collection and create unique decks.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game's price will stay the same throughout Early Access. Upon exiting Early Access, we intend to increase the game's price slightly.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The best way to contact us and to take part in the game's development is to join our Discord server and let us know directly how you feel!
You can also contact us by email or Twitter, we will be reading those attentively!
We are a small team and we're extremely open to constructive criticism, so please do join us and tell us how we can make Breach Wanderers the best game it can be!”

About This Game



Breach Wanderers combines elements from Collectible Card Games and Roguelike Deckbuilders in a brand new way! Create your own decks and choose which cards you will see each time you enter the Breach! With an abundance of creative freedom, choose your hero, create your deck and upgrade your guild before setting off for a new adventure.

Core Features

  • Customize your Deck: Create your own starting deck and card pool before departing for a run. You'll never see a card you don't want to see! Breach Wanderers opens up a world of strategic possibilities to play with when fighting through the breach.
  • RPG Progression: Level-up your heroes to get them prepared for the challenges to come. You can further tune the game to your playstyle with customizable guild upgrades!
  • A Familiar Roguelike Experience: While you can choose how you begin a run, what awaits you in the Breach is different every time! Monsters, special events, items and challenges are all randomized, making every run a unique adventure!

Roadmap

What can you expect from the full game?

  • 8 playable characters, each with their unique skills and unique cards
  • 6 dangerous areas to explore
  • 4 buildings to customize and upgrade your guild
  • Over 500 cards
  • Over 100 items to equip
  • Over 50 monsters to face
  • Over 50 special events to discover
