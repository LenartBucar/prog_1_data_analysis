Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We want to make the game the best we possibly can, and one of the best ways to do that is bring the community closer and actively listen to them. Early Access opens up a direct channel of communication between players and devs: players send us suggestions, ideas, reports, and we act on them and deliver updates as fast as possible. You tell us what you want the game to be, and we make it.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Hadean Tactics will be in Early Access until the first months of 2022.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“For the final version, we are aiming for:
  • The complete roster of 3 Heroes and their unique decks
  • 400+ cards to discover
  • 100+ game changing relics
  • 40+ units to recruit
  • 40+ different demonic enemies
  • 50+ dynamic events
  • All Six Wings of Hell
  • A complete journey of the underworld divided into 3 acts, with 6 different circles to explore
  • And a secret final boss
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“We have two complete playable Heros (with one more already on the way), 150+ cards, 25 units to recruit, 40+ relics, 25 alliances, 17 runes, and 20 events. You can go through all 3 acts, featuring 3 circles of Hell and their respective Wings.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes, the price will be raised when the game leaves Early Access. The final price will reflect the amount of content added since the start of the Early Access process.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to use the Steam Community, Twitter and Discord to get feedback directly from players, answer questions, receive suggestions, and also to update them on new additions in a direct manner, as soon as they go up.
The community can also access the beta branch directly via Steam, testing updates before they are public.”

About This Game



Hadean Tactics is a roguelike deckbuilding game fused with autobattler elements to create a unique strategy experience. Use your cards to influence the actions of your units and help your Hero defeat the Six Wings of Hell.



With procedurally generated paths, an assortment of game changing relics, and a unique battle system that combines the control of deckbuilding with semi real-time combat, no run of Hadean Tactics is ever the same.

The heat of battle

Step into the role of the Summoner and have the spirits of the underworld fight for you as you journey through the different circles of Hell. Arrange your party on the board, use cards to influence their actions in real time, pause whenever you want to, create the best strategies, and guide them to victory.


A selection of characters

Each Hero will offer a different playstyle and different cards to discover during your journey, along with their own unique units that you can recruit and upgrade, expanding your abilities.

Dynamic maps

Explore a new, procedurally-generated path on each run, choosing your own way through the underworld. Discover the six circles of the realm, each with their own challenges and ruled by one of the demonic Six Wings of Hell.

Unlockable artifacts

Each run can unlock new Heroes, relics, cards, and more, expanding your game and giving you something new to discover every time you play.

Mysterious events

Find many events throughout the game that can help you become stronger by making choices, battling special enemies, and more. Uncover the ancient secrets of the underworld, and get powerful rewards to help you on your journey.
