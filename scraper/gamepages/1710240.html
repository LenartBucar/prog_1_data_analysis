About This Content

Starter Pack 2021

A new year is upon us, and with it comes a new assortment of essentials for the fashion-conscious survivor. After all, being stuck out in the unforgiving wilderness is no excuse to stop keeping up appearances!

Starter Pack 2021 Includes:

  • A new playable character, Wanda, the Timekeeper!
    Wanda has arrived just in time! For what? Hm, well she's pretty sure there was something important, now what was it again...


  • Wilson's Snowfallen Skin Set


  • Willow's Victorian Skin Set


  • Walter's Guest of Honor Skin Set


  • Winona's Roseate Skin Set


  • Two new item skins, the Rustic Torch and Splumonkey Sack


  • Yawny Jammies Ensemble, with bonus Yawn emote.
