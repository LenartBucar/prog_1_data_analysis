About This Content

The Space Engineers - Frostbite includes: the Frostbite Scenario, the Antenna Dish, the Dead Engineers, the Gate, the Offset door, the Frozen Armor Skin, the I'm Cold Emote, the Check Wrist Emote and the LCD Posters.

This content is not pay to win and the owner of the package has no advantage against other players. If you decide not to purchase the Frostbite Pack, but you want to play with friends or other players who did buy the Frostbite Pack, you will still be able to join a multiplayer game or server with them and enjoy the game.

Frostbite Scenario

You are a member of a salvage crew, which has been tasked by a mysterious client to acquire something extremely valuable (yet unknown) on Triton. Play solo or with up to four players; explore this moon’s frigid landscape and remarkable architecture, and overcome numerous engineering challenges as you uncover this world’s secrets. Please note, the Frostbite Scenario is in English only.

Antenna Dish

An impressive Antenna Dish used for sending signals across the stars.

Dead Engineers

Six new unique poses for Space Engineer corpses. They can store loot in their backpacks.

Gate

A massive gate that can be used to enhance your ships, vehicles and buildings.

Offset Door

The perfect block to complement your creations.

Frozen Armor Skin

A chillingly beautiful skin that can be used to give blocks a frigid appearance.

I'm Cold Emote

Express to your fellow engineers if you start to struggle in the brutal unforgiving environment of Triton.

Check Wrist Emote

Indicate you are analysing some newly received data with this character animation.

LCD Posters

Featuring iconic advertisements for Cosmic Coffee, Clang Kola, the R.O.S. and Keen Software House.
