About This Content

Texas is a tough fighter designed to fend off enemy attacks and break through enemy defenses. Carries good armor, numerous guns, and very effective AA defense for a Tier V ship.

Advantages:

  • Ten 356 mm guns causing high damage, but with the drawback of quite slow reload time, with a range of fire of 16.4 km.
  • Powerful AA defense with 40 mm Bofors guns.
  • Improved anti-torpedo protection compared to battleships of the same tier.
  • 50% more XP per battle provided by her permanent camouflage.

This package contains:

  • Texas, Tier V American Battleship
  • 1 Port Slot
  • Stars and Stripes Permanent Camouflage
  • 1 Commander with 6 skill points
Confidently steam your way across the dangerous high seas! The last remaining Dreadnought-style capital ship in existence, Texas is an iconic symbol of American naval might. Take her home along with the very American Stars and Stripes permanent camo and a skilled Commander!

The Stars and Stripes camouflage provides the following bonuses:
  • +50% to experience earned in battle
  • −10% to the cost of the ship's post-battle service
  • −3% to surface detectability range
  • +4% to maximum dispersion of shells fired by the enemy at your ship

If you already have this ship in your Port, you will receive 2369 doubloons as compensation.
If you already have this alternative permanent camouflage, you will receive 460 doubloons as compensation.

This DLC is not compatible with an account created outside of Steam.
