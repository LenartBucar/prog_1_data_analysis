Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“In its current state the game is playable from start to finish, but it is still in a "rough around the edges" state. We think Early Access is an excellent way to work on polishing the game experience and shape it around player feedback. We believe that the reviews and feedback we get from our EA players will help us identify what final steps need to be taken in order to make the game shine.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We intend to stay in EA for at least 6 months, but this might change depending on many things.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Apart from the standard bug fixes and quality of life touches to polish the experience, we would like to add more overall content to the final game. This includes potentially adding more locations to explore, items to find or craft and events to encounter. We also plan to implement a few alternative playable characters that would each add their own flavour/twist to the campaign.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game in its current state is playable from start to finish with the standard character/scenario. All the features described in the "About this game" section are implemented. It probably still has some bugs unfortunately but we'll put priority on fixing anything reported by the community.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will be sold at a cheaper price while it stays in Early Access, and will be sold at full price once we complete it.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We'll pay a lot of attention to the reviews and feedback from our players, and we'll use them to take decisions on where to take the game. This should also help us greatly smooth out the game experience, with bug fixes and quality of life changes based on reports from the community.”

About This Game

Card Survival: Tropical Island is a card-based survival experience from the creators of Card Quest.

Experience what life would be like if you were stranded on a desert island, all in a card format.
Explore, craft tools and manage your needs in order to survive in this hostile environment.

Key Features

  • Simple but deep system: all interactions are done by using cards or combining them together.
  • Realistic Simulation: Fight dehydration, starvation, depression and more in a scenario where every decision counts, as dying means starting over.
  • Multiple endings: build yourself a raft to find a way back to civilization, make yourself a more permanent residence on the island or find one of the secret endings.

Many things to do

  • Explore the island to find caves to use as shelter, shipwrecks to scavenge, useful vegetation, water sources and more.
  • Build structures out of wood and mud bricks, make clay containers with a kiln, and eventually complete advanced structures like a water reservoir or a stove for your home.
  • Train skills like spearfishing, climbing, swimming and cooking to improve their chances of success and unlock new cards.

Interesting food and drink mechanics

  • Drink from coconuts, capture rainwater in coconut shells or other containers, collect and purify water from puddles or even extract it from banana trees or solar stills.
  • Many ways to fish: use a fishing rod with different types of bait, try setting some fish traps or learn the art of spearfishing. Smoke or salt your fish meat so that it can last longer.
  • Prepare lots of different foods in your campfire and smoke or salt it so it can last longer. Try to keep a varied diet to avoid saturation and cook complex recipes to keep things entertaining.

Complex health system

  • Make sure you stay happy and motivated to keep your body's defenses up. Entertain yourself by swimming, having deep conversations with imaginary friends, playing music and even making castles in the sand.
  • Suffer from foot and hand sores, calluses, back pain, sunburns, sickness and even bug bites. Craft protective clothing and use multiple medicinal plants to treat these ailments.
  • Fall from trees, slip on rocks, cut yourself, get bitten, gored or step onto urchins and spiders. Wash and dress your wounds and take antibiotics to stave off infection and fever.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 7, 8, 10+
    • Processor: SSE2 instruction set support
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: DirectX11 compatible graphics card
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 500 MB available space
