About This Content

Fill out your stories and dialog even further with Vol 9 of Japanese Anime Voices: Female Character Series. Following the first eight voice packs, this pack will expand your project with more exciting and fun Japanese female anime voices speaking very usable Japanese phrases!

Picking up where Vol 8 left off, this one offers more common, everyday phrases useful for game dialog. Go ahead and give your female players even more things to chat about and expand their dialog!

FEATURES:

* 126 Japanese animation-like female voices (phrases)
* OGG, M4A formats included
* Female voices (in Japanese phrases) for Visual Novel Games, RPG and much more!
* English translation list included
* For use in RPG Maker MV or the engines of your choice!
