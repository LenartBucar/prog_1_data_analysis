Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We would like to go on this journey together with you to get early feedback and make the game even better. Every voice is different and we want as many people as possible to be able to play the game. Your feedback will help us to improve our audio analysis and puzzles to be solvable and fun for everyone. At the same time, you get to see the game grow from the current state to the full version.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The game will be finished in the second half of 2021.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan for the playtime of the full game to be about 5 hours and feature 4 large main areas – Duet Desert, Maestro Mountain, Fogue Forest, and Coda Cave - with an approximate playtime of 1 hour each and 3 smaller sections with a total Playtime of another hour. The planned upcoming updates will also introduce new gameplay mechanics as well as puzzles and before release, we are planning to run a final polish pass over all areas and mechanics.

Similar to the game length we plan for the price to start a bit lower and increase over time. So each player can decide if they want to support the game right from the beginning or join in at a later point. Even if you purchase for a lower price now, you will continue to own the full-priced full version later.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current version of One Hand Clapping already includes the complete first small area (Silent City) and 2 of the larger areas (Duet Desert and Maestro Mountain) as well as the first part of the 3rd main area (Fogue Forest). All of these areas are fully playable at this point.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“One Hand Clapping will start at a slightly lower price during the Early Access period and will increase as more levels are added until the game is completed and the final price is reached.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are eager to receive feedback from the community either on our dedicated Discord channel or here in the discussion forums.”

About This Game


One Hand Clapping is a vocal 2D platformer. Solve puzzles, by singing or humming into your microphone, and find confidence in the power of your voice as it changes the world around you.



One Hand Clapping is a relaxing, inspiring puzzle platformer that focuses on vocal input to progress through its vibrant world. Build confidence in your voice as you utilize melody, rhythm, and harmony as your tools. Take your time. You have nothing to lose and won’t get punished for making a mistake.
Meet loveable characters who will assist you and cheer you on and inspire self-expression without being pushy. You don’t have to be a vocalist prodigy to enjoy One Hand Clapping. Just conquer your doubts, battle the silence, and sing your song.


Just hook up a microphone and you’re good to go!

  • Solve Puzzles with your voice
  • Play through six expansive, vibrant biomes
  • Sing, hum, and beatbox to change the world
  • Meet three whimsical hermits who aid you throughout this musical journey
  • Grow confidence in your voice
  • Sing your song
