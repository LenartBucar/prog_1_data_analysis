Waltz is a growing product and in active development. About This Game

Welcome to the world that makes fantasy feel real and showcases what makes VR unique. Set in a detailed reality full of humor, hidden secrets and interdimensional beings, this top selling VR experience lets you wield incredible superpowers in ways that anyone can enjoy.

An incredibly diverse showcase of VR’s potential for new and experienced users alike, with features like mic input, gesture recognition, interactive characters and more.

Features

  • Intuitive Spell Casting using natural movements and mic input
  • Tons of Secrets, Dark Humor and Puzzles to discover and solve
  • Sandbox environments with hundreds of unique objects, mechanics and interactions
  • Massive 40-level Fortress with hostile opponents, traps and hidden mysteries


Waltz of the Wizard is actively being expanded as Aldin continues pioneering novel software technologies that make you truly feel present in an alternate reality.


Natural Magic adds a massive collection of new content and replayability, introducing a unique magic physics engine that’s entirely new to VR, conjured and controlled through natural movement as if magic were real. Venture into the fortress to recover lost artifacts from beings that harness magic to control time and space, or have fun in a friendly wizard tower and courtyard full of strange oddities.


Aldin has been pushing the limits of VR for years and is actively developing more content for Natural Magic, continuing the mission to make you truly feel present in a believable reality, pioneering simulations, interactive characters, hand-tracking and gestures for a unique and immersive experience.


Cast creative and diverse spells in a fully interactive world, discovering the many details of a world designed to feel real. Combine arcane ingredients into a boiling cauldron with the help of an ancient spirit trapped in a human skull. Travel to new places, finding yourself in mysterious circumstances full of detail and unforgettable atmosphere.
