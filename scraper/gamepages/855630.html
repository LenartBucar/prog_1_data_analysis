About This Game

As a result of the experiment, the main character of the game, Maxx goes through a portal gate from Earth to an amazing planet where everlasting summer reigns. A planet with tropical forests and no highways. A planet where sakura does not bloom, but TWIT grows. A planet, where there are seductive assassins and kings' tyranny. Here you can found not only cat-girls, but even purple hamsters, who have enormous power.
Was it his fault or some divine intervention? Why did he agree to the experiment? Can he go back or just starlight will remind him of home? Will he enjoy wonderful everyday here or die ignominiously?
Past life is gone forever and you need to solve the riddles of the new planet and build relationships with new friends. In this world, everything is not so simple and Maxx needs to decide who he can trust and who can stab him in the back. Gradually, the question how to get back is less and less relevant for our hero.


Our game is a unique mix of two styles. You'll get opening and closing arts in real-style and the rest game in anime. A colorful and dynamic adult story about the adventures of special forces soldier Maxx in the mysterious world of Zeliria. Zeliria... A dangerous, still beautiful planet, where neko live side by side with knights.
A game where you decide the fate of the hero.





Our visual novel combines fantasy, drama, romance, humor, thriller, and of course beautiful girls and fluffy (not)humster.
Where did the squid come from?!
