Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We want to get the core game play into the hands of the community as quickly as possible. Since the game is primarily multiplayer, it’s important that we gather feedback on the pacing, balance, mini games, puzzle and item mechanics from a larger player base”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We are expecting between 8 and 10 months, this will depend on the changes we need to make based on community feedback; as well as the new features and content we need to add to the game.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We will continue to add more content to the game including more themes (maps), more mini games, more ways to customize your character. We will also be adding daily rewards, a discord integration to make multiplayer teaming easier. We will be adding more game mechanics through traps or item functionality, responding to player demand. More player rewards through an Achievement system and by taking advantage of Steam’s Achievements. Finally, we are looking into having an Artificial Intelligence driven Infected for players to compete against.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“We have the core game play implemented, with basic matchmaking for your friends and groups, tons of Infected Skins and Customizations, as well as a Custom Game Mode to group with your friends and experiment with changing the settings of the game.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We expect the game price will be the same, but purchasers of Early Access may start with more currency, as a thank you for being an early adopter.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to gather feedback from various Social channels, we will be using the Steam Forums to communicate and listen to feedback. We also plan on working with Streamers and Content Creators on YouTube & other platforms.”

About This Game

"The PIGGY universe expands with PIGGY: HUNT, a multiplayer adventure where survivors team up to escape the wrath of the Infected, the monstrosity trying to hunt them down.

Survivors

Survivors complete tasks, solve puzzles, find and use tools to help one another survive and escape!

Infected

Infected use traps and all of your senses to hunt down the Survivors!


Features:
• Fast-paced fun for up to 6 players; more players available with custom-game mode!
• Highly customizable characters, weapons, and items.
• Use custom-game mode to create endless playable options.
• 3 House maps to play, each with different mechanics to win.
• Officially licensed game from MiniToon.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 10
    • Processor: Intel Core I3 8th generation
    • Memory: 2 GB RAM
    • Graphics: GeForce GTX 750
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 500 MB available space
    Recommended:
    • OS: Windows 10
    • Processor: Intel Core i3 10th generation
