Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We have always placed great value on feedback from the community. With Indoorlands we want to go one step further and develop the game in cooperation with the community. The early access approach is perfect for this. We want to let you participate in the development and learn how you play Indoorlands, which ideas and features you like and which bugs need to be fixed. Since Indoorlands is our first park management game, we need your support.

Attention: Early Access should give you a first taste of the game. You should not play Indoorlands in Early Access if you expect a fully developed game. There is still a lot of work to do. If you want to play a complete, fully developed game, you should wait for the release of version 1.0.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“In principle, we don't want to make any promises we can't keep. For this reason, and because the duration of development also depends directly on your feedback & ideas, it is not easy to name a concrete time frame. We currently calculate with a period of 6-12 months. But please don't nail us down on that. It's done when it's done :)”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Until the release as a full version, we will implement various improvements, features and more content. Check out the roadmap to get to know more.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current version of Indoorlands should run stable and offers a lot of content:
  • 9 rides
  • 15 Hall types
  • Over 700 Decoration objects from 8 theming options
  • 11 Wall paintings
  • Over 20 stores, restaurants and entertainment halls
  • An economic and satisfaction system
  • Configurable stores and restaurants (price vs performance)
  • 3 different transport systems with a total of 20 expansion stages
  • Workshop support
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes! we would like to thank players who buy our game in early access and support us in its development with a discounted price. Since we set a high value on transparency and fairness, we would like to inform you about the different planned prices for the different stages of development:
  • EA Phase: 13.99$
  • Full version: 19,99$

We will inform you about upcoming price changes in the news.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community will play an important role in the development process. As with our previous projects, we place great value on player feedback.

This will happen in direct exchange on our Discord Server, via the Steam Community Forums as well as via our feedback form.

Bug reports, concrete ideas, as well as general feedback on game mechanics and feel, will be included in the regular updates until the full version is released.

Our goal is to publish a game that is fun for rides enthusiasts, amusement park lovers and casual gamers alike. Last but not least the balancing is crucial, and we depend on your feedback to optimize it.”

About This Game

Welcome to your own indoor amusement park


Build your own indoor amusement park, manage it and convince your visitors with individually designed halls and rides that you can control yourself. Will you manage to exceed the needs of your visitors? You are the boss: Let your creativity run free and run your own customized park that fully immerse your guests into another world.

Build your dream amusement park


Build, expand, and manage your own park. Take up the challenge and progressively unlock different types of halls, rides, decorations and shops. Level up your buildings, choose individual perks and adjust price/quality, so your visitors are ultimatively satisfied.


You can choose from a variety of themes and combine them freely - there are no limits to your creativity. Design your park piece by piece: Indoorlands makes a designer out of you!

Control and ride unique flatrides


Your rides are the center of attention at Indoorlands. Not only can you control them and paint them individually, you can also record and save your own ride programs. Choose from your different ride programs and let them play automatically.


Turn your dreams into reality


Show the world your vision of a perfect indoor park. Indoorlands offers every player the opportunity to create a unique park. Place and decorate your ride halls and decoration halls. Design your rides and create breathtaking ride programs. Choose between different stores and restaurants, make your visitors happy and become the most successful park entrepreneur.
Whether you want to recreate your favorite parkrides or leave the real world at the door when designing your rides... - the decision is up to you!
