About This Content

Listen to the award winning soundtrack, containing additional cutscenes music and complete in-game music from Kingdom Come: Deliverance.

OST contains all the music from the game which was not included in the "OST Essentials". You will find here more cutscenes music, all in-game music tracks as well as some bonus contents, such as the instrumental versions of the two songs, trailers and more.

This OST contains 175 mp3 files, subdivided into five albums:

  • Disk 2: More cutscenes from the game
  • Disk 3: Even more cutscenes from the game
  • Disk 4: Medieval pubs and chants, minigames, and combat
  • Disk 5: Music from the towns
  • Disk 6: Countryside, times of day, weather

We also included some playlists for your convenience. Their filenames describe which music is contained in them. "All Tracks, incl. OST Essentials.m3u" playlist assumes you have also purchased the OST Essentials. If you have, you should see the mp3s from both DLCs in this directory.

TOTAL TIME 4 hours, 09 minutes, 55 seconds

Produced by Jan Valta & Adam Sporka
