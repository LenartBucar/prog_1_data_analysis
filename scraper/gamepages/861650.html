Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Although we have our long term vision and roadmap for Session, we felt that Early Access was the perfect platform for us to get the game into the players hands with a core experience as soon as possible and to get important feedback earlier on.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We don't believe in never ending Early Access cycles and plan to officially release as soon as we can all the while making sure to leverage player feedback and iterations on gameplay as much as possible, until we feel we have a solid release.

That being said, we will most likely be in Early Access somewhere between 6-12 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The final version of the game will include the complete NYC level as well as many other features that will be added progessively to the game through major and minor updates during the Early Access period.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Currently, our Early Access build contains core features that we felt were necessary to include from the start in order to give the player a solid enough experience as well as allow us to get feedback on important aspects. However, this is only a subset of what we plan to have for the final version of the game.

Although we try our best to make sure there are no "game breaking" issues, the game is still in developement therefore it will unfortunately not be without issues. However, we plan on addressing bugs and issues and ideally get minor updates (bug fixes, small features, gameplay changes, etc.) out on a regular basis.

Creating any game, especially a skateboarding game is complex, time consuming and takes time to get it right. One example of this is our Free Grind System. Initially, we implemented our grind system using splines / grind bounding volumes. Those would tell the player they were on a grind and the general direction of the grind. That system worked, but wasn't without its problems and shortcomings. We decided to scrap splines and grind bounding volumes altogether and implemented a Free Grind System which basically means, if you can see it, can get to it, you can grind it! Needless to say, this is a much much more complex system to implement and is still a work in progress so bare with us. However, it's already in great shape and the advantages are completely worth it as it offers the players opportunities that we hadn't even seen or thought of as we were laying out our level design, not to mention many other development benefits.

THIS is freedom of expression!”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price will change during the course of the Early Access period. With every major update we plan on increasing the price as well as one final time for the full release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We have currently been involving the community ever since our prototype launch on Kickstarter and plan on continuing to do this through the Steam community, our social media platforms and our discord server.”

About This Game

"Inspired by the golden era of skateboarding, Session's primary goal is to make you experience what skateboarding really is; an incredible culture where there are no other goals other than expressing your creativity and achieving success through hard work, perseverance and bits of madness for no one else other than yourself.

Be ready to play the most authentic and grounded skateboarding experience in the most legendary spots on earth, film and make the gnarliest skate parts out there.

True Stance Stick controls: Don’t be fooled by copycats and experience the real pioneering dual stick board control feature! Session offers for the first time a per foot stick control giving you the full power in your hands where each stick represents a foot. Careful though, skating switch now has a real meaning.

Filming has always been a big part of the skateboarding culture. With Session's video editor players can perform their tricks; edit, and create a montage in-game.

Explore the best and most iconic skate spots, starting with New-York which helped shape what street skating is today. Recreated on a 1:1 scale and supported by grounded and realistic skate physics, get the feel of what it is to skate the infamous Brooklyn Banks and our fresh twist of the surrounding financial district of Manhattan."
