About This Content

Iron-clad and muscle-bound, these brutal Orruks are trapped in an endless battle within the shifting city of Shadespire. They wield huge, rusted weapons and thirst for the crash and crunch of combat. Unlock this warband and play as the Ironskull’s Boyz in Warhammer Underworlds: Online now.

THE WARBAND

  • Hitting hard and tough to kill, this fearsome warband is comprised of four savage fighters.
  • The Orruks inspire when they take damage - so they thrive where the fighting is thickest!
  • When Gurzag, the leader of this warband, is inspired, he is able to re-roll one dice when he makes an attack action!

TIPS & TRICKS

  • While this warband may lack mobility and sheer weight of numbers, each fighter is a serious close combat threat. Upgrades to their mobility, survivability, or damage output are very potent.
  • Consider bringing cards which trigger immediate extra Attack actions, and any that allow you to affect fighter positioning to take advantage of how hard this warband hits.
  • The Orruks inspire when wounded, and not just from enemy attacks. Running through a lethal hex on your way to shell out damage will also trigger your fighters to inspire!

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: 64-bit Windows 7, 64-bit Windows 8 (8.1) or 64-bit Windows 10
    • Processor: Intel Core i5-2300 @ 2.80 GHz / AMD FX-4350 @ 4.2 GHz
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 or AMD Radeon HD 5870
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 10 GB available space
