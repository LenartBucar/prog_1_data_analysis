About This Content

The Recon Shortcut Kit gives you the boost you need to take down your opponents with instant access to Recon progression upgrades, specializations, and weapons. Whether you’re new to Battlefield 4 or just want to make up for lost time, Shortcut Kits help you go toe-to-toe with Battlefield veterans by instantly unlocking powerful gear or bonuses that would otherwise be obtained through play.

7 gadgets and weapon accessories. Track enemy movement using the Motion Sensor, ambush your enemies with mines, and create tactical spawn points for your team with the Radio Beacon.

3 class specializations. There's no such thing as a one-size-fits-all approach to battle. Become a front line powerhouse with the Offensive Spec, hunt your enemies from the shadows using the Spec Ops Spec, and dominate from afar with the Sniper Spec.

7 sniper rifles. Not every encounter needs to be up close and personal. As a Recon player, it's your job to safeguard objectives, spot and track enemies, and take them out before they ever even know you're around.

Note: this Shortcut Kit does not include any gadgets or weapons added by expansion packs.
