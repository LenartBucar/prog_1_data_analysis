About This Content

Fed up with your staff looking like professionals? Well, inject a bit of vim, a squeeze of vigour, and a blast of class into your hospital attire!

It’s time to pull back the dressing room curtain on some of the fanciest frockery in town. Welcome, kind people, to the snappily named “Two Point Hospital: Fancy Dress Pack”.

Mesmerise your patients by diagnosing them as a wizard! Shock the staff room by sauntering in as a fish! Or horrify everyone by walking out of the toilet dressed as a zombie!
Whatever you’re after, whether it be tangy outfits or outrageous headwear, we have something to tickle your fancy.

Here’s what’s included!

Outfits:


  • Scurvy Dog
  • Dying Trend
  • Something Fishy
  • Groovy Garb
  • Disco Shawl
  • Leisure Flare
  • Spicy Smock

  • Penguin Suit

Headwear:
