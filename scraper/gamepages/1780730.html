About This Game

Then from now on... Do I have to defeat the devil?

The main character and Lee Hyun-ji, who were ordinary office workers who had nothing to do with this world.
The two were transferred to the world by summoning Eugene Arc, the emperor of the Esperado Empire.
Adventure was all I've ever done on a computer! Defeat the devil safely and return to Earth!

Function and Configuration Guidance =======================
It is operated as a stage-type story selection and turn-based battle, and characters can be grown through reinforcement.
Get a colleague by lifting the stage, and go on an adventure to defeat the Four Heavenly Kings and the Devil.
A total of 13 different endings are prepared, and the player's choice may lead to something hidden.

Overview =======================
Genre: Miyeon's and turn-based strategies.
System: Character growth through potion, 3:3 PVE with monsters through turn-based battles.
Composition: Organizational story reading and replaying through stage selection method.

Guidance on the connection with my previous work =======================
The game has an indirect connection with "One Summer Day's Story," "Grand Empire," and "Lost Report."
However, all three have only brought some of the characters or settings, and there is little connection with this work.
It is designed to be enjoyed regardless of the need to play the previous one, and we prepared it with the utmost care so that there is no sense of incompatibility even if you don't know the previous one.
