About This Game

Flash will no longer be supported on December 31, 2020, and the era of flash will come to an end.Now, the classic flash game series CrazyFlasher, which used to accompany young people, is packed by its author Andy law and uploaded to the world - famous game platform Steam, which is convenient for players to collect.

This collection contains the Chinese and English versions of 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 in the CrazyFlasher series.

Crazy Flasher 2
It's an action fighting game.
Contrl:
Move:WSAD （Run:double-click A or D）
Atack:B
Jump:N
Special:M
Weapon:Space

Crazy Flasher 3
It's an action fighting and shooting game.You can buy powerful weapons.
Contrl:
Move:WSAD （Run:double-click A or D）
Atack:B
Jump:N
Special:M
Weapon:Space

Crazy Flasher 4
It's an action shooting game.You can drive.You can buy many guns.
Contrl:
Run: WASD
Shoot: Mouse
Change weapons: 1234
Reload: R
Drop weapon: G
Map: E
Save game: Q
Buy things (near the shopgirl): B
Talk to NPC / get in car: SPACE
Game menu: M

Crazy Flasher 5
It's an action shooting game.You can drive.You can buy many guns.You can have two NPC help you to fight.
Contrl:
WASD control the character's movement(press jump after squat will make you jump higher, squat and use left and right is quick-rolling ), use your mouse to control the attack direction, left click is to attack. To switch weapons you use 1234 and button E. R is to reload; G is to drop your weapon, and button Q is to see your current condition and game setups. Space is to switch to communicate or drive. Press B in the assigned area is to buy weapons. Right click while playing can command your teammates or Save game.

Crazy Flasher 6
It's an action shooting game.You can drive.You can buy a few guns.You can shoot and fight at the same time.
Contrl:
WASD: Move. J: attack. K: change clip. B: Buy Weapons. H: Help.
When the enemy is close, press J to attack with knife. You can be attacked after being captured by the bad guy. Double-click the base to speed up. There are different attacks when you press J or K.
Earthquake(lose a little HP):j+k
Throw grenades (cost MP): ↓ + → + K
Somersault (it could win a little HP after successfully hit the enemy, cost MP) :→ + ↓ + K
Straight (cost MP): ↓ + ↑ + K
