Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“I have been working on this game for a little longer than 3 years. I would like to gather some user feedback to help me finalize the game to ensure that the game is up to quality expectations.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I am aiming to have the game in early access for the few upcoming months to implement player feedbacks and further polish the game. However, the core gameplay will stay intact.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The game will receive minor balance and feature updates that I will put in according to community feedback. These updates will allow me to fine-tune the game by getting user feedback. As a result, the full version of the game would provide more polished experience while still keeping the core gameplay element intact.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is currently in beta stage. It is totally playable on its own at the moment, but at this point, there is no ending to the game, much like most of the simulation games that are available on the steam store. Please refer to the "about this game" section of the store page for more details.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“I plan to increase the price of the game by 10~20% when the final build is released.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I am releasing Victory Road as an early access title because I want more user feedback. I want to actively involve the players by looking at the comments/discussions to see what needs to be improved. I would also like to have a sort of weekly gathering for a discussion so I can see how the players feel about our game so far. I may also do some polls and see what people want the most and what can be totally omitted.

I encourage you guys to leave any sort of feedback(this includes criticisms) you have on the game so that I can look over them and make improvements to the game.”

About This Game


In Victory Road, players will assume the role of a boxing coach and meticulously plan each day’s training and meals. Players will be able to customize their boxer through adjusting his training routine to emphasize the growth of specific stats, altering his fighting style, and changing his equipment and special techniques. Matches take place in real-time using a fighting game-inspired engine, and can be won or lost depending on the advice shouted to your boxer. Boxers will gradually learn unique fighting styles, combos, and techniques depending on how the player guides him.




  • Build your boxer up into a champion with the right training and diet. Mastering the interactions between nutrition and exercise is the key to success.
  • Matches play out in real-time, utilizing a custom fighting game engine and AI system.
  • Being on the sideline during a match doesn’t mean you’ve got nothing to do! Yell advice to your boxer, pound on the mat to get him fired up, or even slip him a little something to give him an extra little edge!
  • Switch between three major fighting styles to streamline your strategy and take advantage of boxer’s stats!
  • Skill tree, combo system, and attack familiarity system allow you to customize your boxer’s fighting tactics and create your own unique boxing style.
  • Discover combos through telling the boxer to use certain punches or maneuvers during matches! Turn the tide of the battle!
  • Conquer all 4 leagues available in the game by fighting against over 150 different boxers with different strategies and builds.
  • Coaching isn’t just about boxing! Sell hotdogs, advertise your gym, or even toil in the mines; You’ll do whatever it takes to succeed!
