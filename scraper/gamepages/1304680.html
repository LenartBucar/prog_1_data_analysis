What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“While the early access version of Voidigo is filled with content, we want your help and feedback during development as we aim to leave early access within a year or so.

We are committed to updating the game regularly, and with a very transparent development process through our livestreams and community.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We aim to be out of early access in 2022 but we also plan to support the development post-release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version of Voidigo will have more content across the board. More worlds, meta progression, weapons, bosses and powerups. But also a complete story!”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of Voidigo already has multiple worlds, many weapons and powerups. As well as several unique bosses.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Because we want to build a strong community around the game it will be cheaper during the early access period.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We have a discord server and weekly livestreams of the development for the community to join in on. We really want to release a great product for everyone and are always open for feedback!

About This Game

Voidigo is a chaotic and colorful roguelite shooter with a focus on dynamic boss fights.


Equip one of the many ranged weapons and fight back the corruption.



Or use one of the melee weapons if you prefer a more personal approach.



Dodge attacks and stomp your enemies to gain the upper hand.




The many bosses of Voidigo will put up a good fight, relentlessly fueled by the void's corruption.



During the fight the boss might flee the area, leaving you to decide whether to hunt them, or gather your strength.



But don't linger too long or they will start hunting you.




To help you on your fight, find a wide array of powerups for fun synergies.



When between worlds, trade your weapons, powerups and other resources. To fully tailor your playstyle.
