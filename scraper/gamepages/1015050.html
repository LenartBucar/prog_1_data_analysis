About This Content

The final DLC for the all Rabi-Ribi fans! Come challenging new Special Bosses with Erina and Ribbon in new costumes! Also a new playable character!

Now with Cocoa Mode! Now you can play as Cocoa in speedrun mode!*

  • Cocoa have a complete different play style compare to Erina!
  • Cocoa found a use for all of Erina's items! All inputs are the same as Erina's but with different effects (for example, Cocoa will throw a teleport bomb with "Sliding Powder", instead of doing a slide)
  • Ribbon's magic weapons allow Cocoa to use different types of Cocoa Bombs.
  • Cocoa's got herself some new hidden techs as she could not perform some of them.
  • HUD and some artworks now in Cocoa style
  • Cocoa Achievements!
*Clear Chapter 8 in any mode and difficulty as Erina to unlock Cocoa Mode. (OR Unlock Speedrun mode by pressing "MAGIC CHANGE RIGHT" + "UP" on title screen, and Clear Chapter 5 in any mode and difficulty as Erina to unlock Cocoa Mode.)

DLC Contents

  • Playable Cocoa in Speedrun Mode
  • 1 new costume for Erina
  • 4 new costumes for Ribbon including Halloween Ribbon, "Ending Scene" Ribbon and more
  • 4 extra CGs added into the main story
  • New SPECIAL Bosses including SPECIAL Irisu, Rita and more*
  • New achievements


*Bosses' difficulty are balanced without "Is the order a DLC?" items.
*Requires finishing Post-Game to access
