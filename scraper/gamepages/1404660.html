Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“In order to bring the experience of the current product to the players and to be able to react to feedback as early as possible, we would like to offer the Early Access Status. This allows players to get an impression of the game before it is finished and even participate in it. So the Early Access Status is there to offer a product where we are still working on.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“If everything goes according to plan and well, the full release is scheduled for October 2021 at the latest.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full game will include a much longer story. In addition, the map will be further expanded, the story-telling synchronized and new features added.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current build offers:
– The complete exploration of the map
– A basic story
– Side stories
– Tasks”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“At the beginning of the Early Access phase the game will have a lower price than at the release. The reason for this is that the game is simply not finished yet. We do not want to charge a full amount for something that is not yet finished. When the game is released, the price of the game will increase a bit.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community is involved in every change and can send us feedback and suggestions in the Steam Discussions, via email or social media like Twitter and Instagram. In addition, particularly active followers receive pre-access to the beta and can test it together with us.”

About This Game



Psych is a psychological horror thriller with a big focus on a suspenseful, hand-written story and an unforgettable atmosphere. Through unique, self-developed systems, Psych offers a new and innovative gaming experience that will not be forgotten. The open world invites you to explore, if you dare. Every corner of the park is filled with stories, puzzles or secrets to uncover. However, the biggest mystery is what happened on the day of the accident with Alex. While the main story has a few ways to unravel the mysteries, the side stories also provide plenty of clues and information. Psych is divided into 5 chapters that advance the story immersively, without the player noticing. The customized and countless sound effects will give you goosebumps after goosebumps. With Psych we want to create real horror, a game that will shake up the horror genre. Here in detail the main features of Psych:

  • An emotional, thrilling and unforgettable main story divided into 5 chapters.
  • Plenty of side stories, puzzles and secrets to uncover
  • A mysterious and dark atmosphere you won't forget
  • Consequences based on your decisions
  • A dynamic and interactable world to explore
  • A custom made system to experience horror in a new way
  • A dozen of goosebump-inducing sound effects
  • Realistic, modern and stunning graphics

Average play time of the current version: 50 minutes
Planned play time of all chapters: 3 hours



You are a murderer. You, Alexander Green, are a goddamn murderer. Now you're finally out of the asylum, and what have you learned from it? Not a damn thing! That your guilt is chasing you into your dreams is something you deserve. You shouldn't even be alive. Look at you, a hopeless man, wandering the streets, without any sense of life. What do you want to do now? Do you want to run away or face your own enemy, yourself?

Experience the story of Alexander Green and find out what happened on the day of the accident. Follow the development of Alex, solve puzzles around the event and try not to fall into madness.



Nothing is as it seems. Something or someone is following you. You must always be on your guard and be prepared for possible dangers. Especially at night, it can be quite dangerous when you see things you'd rather not see.

Unstoppable tension. Not a minute will pass when you can feel safe. New clues will keep coming up and will tempt you to take another step. No step is like the other and leads to ever new exits.

Scary horror atmosphere at every step of the game will make you fear. Whether you are prepared for it or not. There is no one who will not be afraid here.

Self-developed systems that control the horror and your state of mind bring a new, intuitive and exciting way to play this psychological horror thriller.

A small pinch of coincidence to the emotional and harrowing story of Psych brings each player a personalised gaming experience that can differ from time to time.

Dozen side stories, puzzles and secrets to discover in the open world of Psych. Each with its own unique story that will captivate you.



Goose-pimple sound effects will send a cold shiver down your spine. If you think that you have already seen and, above all, heard everything, you will be mistaken. There are so many tailor-made sound effects, perfectly tuned to the atmosphere, that will take your breath away again and again.

Fascinating graphics through modern technology and a little bit of dexterity, additionally provides the right thrill while playing and takes you on an unforgettable journey through the world and story of Alexander Green.
