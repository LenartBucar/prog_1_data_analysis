About This Content

HUMANKIND Original Soundtrack is the new orchestral album from Arnaud Roy, the composer already at work on the Endless series. Including all orchestral music from the game.

The Humankind music is an ode to humanism, transmission and multiculturalism. One and a half-hour of epic orchestral music, featuring traditional instruments from around the world, baroque instruments and choir in ancient Greek or Latin, to create an unprecedented mix of cultures. The soundtrack has been performed by the Slovak National Symphony Orchestra and a roster of astounding musicians.

19 tracks, 88 mn. Includes MP3 and FLAC files.
