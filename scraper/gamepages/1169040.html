Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Plans for Necesse are not set in stone. The game will grow in content and features with the help from community feedback as it gets closer to release. Early Access will be a great way to invite players into the development and creative direction of the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Even though I hope to keep working on the game for a long time after release, with the current plans I approximate 12 months to reach a state of content, features and polish where I can call it complete.

With community involvement, it is possible the game will have additional unplanned features that will extend this time period.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“A completed state of the game will be much more polished and include features like modding support with workshop, better optimization, support for Mac and Linux, difficulty options and balance, more advanced settlements, bosses, biomes, monsters and much more. The list of planned features for release is subject to change with community feedback.
Even with the game released, I hope to keep working on and update the game.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is fully playable with a decent amount of content. You can play solo, with friends and host a server. Most mechanics are already implemented, leaving more content and features to be created relatively quickly. A list of features currently in the game can be found below, under the "About This Game" section.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“It's possible the price will increase as the game gets more polished, more content and approaches release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community feedback will be crucial in the direction and development of the game. From fixing bugs to ideas for new features and content. Use the Steam Discussion Boards or the official Discord Server to provide feedback.”

About This Game

Start your adventure and explore the infinite procedural generated world of Necesse. Mine, fight and craft to gear up and conquer powerful enemies that drop unique items. Set sail to visit other islands containing villages, dungeons and other secrets or start your own settlement by building houses for settlers to move into.


Travel alone or invite your friends as companions

The game can be played solo or with multiple players from the beginning, and progress through scaled bosses to the end. Your character will never be locked to one build. Choose your weapon and gear to fight, change it up at any time, or mix and match to find the best setup for the situation.


Go underground

Mine ores and find treasure to craft better armor and weapons. Explore the world of different bosses, monsters, biomes, villages, dungeons and more.


Build your own settlements

Manage settlement NPC's and trade items with them to get better gear in preparation for the next challenge. Build a farm to supply yourself with powerful potions and buffs that will help you progress through the game.




Other features include:
  • Find, collect and style up with cosmetics, pets and mounts.
  • Create circuit networks and traps to kill incoming enemies.
  • Host a dedicated server for you and your friends to play on.
