About This Content

Patron - Supporter Pack offers an assortment of digital-only goodies for players who want to make an extra step in supporting Patron and us at Overseer Games.

Everything in this pack is cosmetic only and has no effect on gameplay in any way.

The pack includes:
  • Patron's Villa – a special in-game building
  • Patron's garden - a special in-game beautification
  • Patron's banner - a special in-game beautification
  • 5 additional banners to choose from when starting a new game
  • Print-ready Patron and Overseer Games logos (.eps & .ai formats)
