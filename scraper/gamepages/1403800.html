About This Software

Meet Movavi Picverse

– your new AI-based helper that will take your photo editing to the next level. Enhance your photos any way you want: adjust sharpness and color, restore old images, remove unwanted objects, change backgrounds – and do it all in a couple of clicks.

AI-Powered Toolkit

Movavi Picverse uses artificial intelligence to edit your photos, and that is why it is so fast.

Here’s what you can do:
  • Adjust colors automatically with Magic Enhance or use manual controls
  • Apply HDR lighting to balance contrast and emphasize details
  • Remove digital noise caused by inadequate lighting conditions
  • Restore old photos: remove scratches and revive colors

Smart Resizing Algorithms

Whether you need to compress or scale up an image, you can do it without losing image quality. Choose the size you need, and AI will take care of the rest.

One-Click Retouching

Give portraits and selfies a total makeover using image retouching tools:
  • Reshape faces and bodies
  • Remove red-eye
  • Whiten teeth
  • Smooth out skin, remove shine, wrinkles, and other blemishes

Object Removal

Delete unwanted stuff from your pictures using the handy Stamp tool. Copy “clean” areas to paint over problematic ones.


Background Removal

Replace the original background with a different picture or remove the background altogether. AI identifies people in photographs and selects their silhouettes automatically.

More Tools

Movavi Picverse also comes in handy for basic editing. You can have fun with dozens of filters and effects, add text, crop and rotate images, and straighten the horizon.


Flexible Formats

You can work with any graphics format, including RAW. When exporting the resulting photo, you can choose the output format and size: JPEG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, DPX, PBM, and more.
