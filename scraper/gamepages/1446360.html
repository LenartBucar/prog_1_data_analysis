About This Content

Rediscover the feeling of real-time strategy nostalgia wherever you are with the official Age of Empires III: Definitive Edition soundtrack. This soundtrack includes all 101 songs from one of the most beloved and best-selling real-time strategy games of all time, remastered for a modern listening experience.

The files are 48Khz 24bit stereo WAV files.
