Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Almighty is a combination of loot grabbing and home base building, combined with a very social experience. We want to take advantage of all the wonderful tools Steam provides us, and include the Steam community, as we wrap up our development to ensure that the overall game is something that is tied together well. We're looking for feedback that we can act upon to ensure the game as a whole feels good with numerous people playing.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We have a development roadmap we'll be sharing soon, but we're aiming for around 7 - 12 months in Steam Early Access.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“If all goes according to plan, the final version of Almighty will include more Home Island customization, more enemy islands to explore, more loot, more powers to use, more enemies to battle, expanded clan and social features, and entirely new gameplay features we're not ready to reveal yet. We'll also be aiming to improve upon any bugs or places of friction for the overall user experience.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The core features of Almighty are all available: 4-player multiplayer, a clan system, a dozen islands available to be explored and looted, over 30 Power Stones to equip, a dozen amulet spells to cast, Home Island upgrading and customization, Home Island raids, rotating weekly quests and challenges, character customization, dungeons and much more. Our Home Island system is in place however it will likely need some player feedback to ensure the act is both fun and rewarding.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes! We're keenly aware that we haven't finished development and we're asking the Steam community to contribute their time to help us shape the game so we're going to have a lower price during Early Access, and we'll be at a higher price at 1.0.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are wanting to engage the steam community for feedback, opinions, and ideas to help make Almighty a better experience for everyone.”

About This Game


Hunt demons, monsters, and gods solo or online with friends in this action RPG. You are an Alpha, a magical warrior infused with the power of your ancestors.

Run, leap and fly across islands, ripping your foes to pieces, and rebuilding your shattered Home Island with their remains. Bring low the oppressive false gods, protect your kindred and become Almighty.



  • Use magic gauntlets for devastating ranged or melee attacks, and amulets to summon powerful spells in this 3rd person Action RPG.
  • Play in solo or co-operative online play with up to 4 players.
  • Craft and customize powerful gear to create your own unique character class and play styles.
  • Upgrade and defend your Home Island, a strong home gives you power. Visit your friends’ Home Islands and help each other upgrade and defend from enemy raids.



  • Fight in reactive, changing environments. Anger the gods and see the islands change before your eyes.
