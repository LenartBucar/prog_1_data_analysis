Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early Access is a period in which players can purchase Industries of Titan early if they would like to support development, help shape its future, or simply experience the game before 1.0. During this time, we’ll continue to add features, balance the game based on player feedback and further polish the game.

Early Access allows us to get your feedback as early as possible so we can make Industries of Titan the best game it can be. We’re bursting with ideas and want you to help us shape the future of the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Our very rough estimate is one year, but that is subject to change. We will continue to develop Industries of Titan in Early Access with monthly updates until we feel that it has reached a 1.0 state.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to add features that will add more complexity and replayability such as competing against rival corporations, a tech tree, additional buildings, ships, combat devices, simulation layers and more! You can always check our roadmap on our store page or our website to see what's in the pipeline.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“In its current state, Industries of Titan has most of its core gameplay mechanics ready regarding city building, population management, factory gameplay, and combat. The current gameplay offers a sturdy framework for us to build upon with the many robust and exciting updates we have yet to come.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We do not currently have plans to price Industries of Titan differently after Early Access, but this could change if we continue to update Titan for longer than we originally planned.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Your feedback will shape the future of Titan! We’d love to hear your feedback and there are several places you can reach us to do that; first, you can submit feedback through the in-game bug reporter. You can also find us on Discord (linked on our store page), head over to our Steam Forums or find us on Twitter and Reddit.”

About This Game

Industries of Titan is an industrial city building sim/strategy game set on Saturn's moon, Titan. Create a sprawling industrial city, design powerful factories, and compete with other rival corporations to stake your claim to the industries of Titan!



As the Founder of a new city-corporation, and backed by the unforgiving and mysterious Council, you've been granted new headquarters in Titan's inhospitable atmosphere. There, you'll need to bear the onslaught of ruthless rebels while keeping your benefactor happy and explore the ruins of a forgotten civilization. The Council expects much from you. Do you have what it takes to truly thrive?

BUILD AND MANAGE YOUR CITY

On Titan, precious construction materials and technological artifacts are locked away in ancient, massive ruins. Use these materials and artifacts to construct roads and transportation, build residences for citizens, power your city, and generate profits.


  • Design your city, and expand from just a few devices to a massive metropolis!
  • Survey and salvage the ruins of a lost civilization for ancient artifacts
  • Balance the needs of the Council, your city, your citizens, and your employees to produce a powerful, efficient economy

DIVE INTO YOUR FACTORIES

Some buildings, like factories, are special in that they have internal grids where you can build devices that act as cheaper, but less powerful versions of their building-sized counterparts. They’re not only a great way to kickstart your economy before you unlock more expensive buildings, but an essential way to grow your wealth.


  • Build a variety of devices inside your factories for smaller scale investments
  • Set up processors that can refine your resources to exponentially increase their value
  • Get creative with your factory layouts to optimize floor efficiency

STRIKE DOWN YOUR ENEMIES

Your city’s success will undoubtedly draw the attention and ire of those around you. You will have to fight the rebel forces and defend your corporate assets to become the dominant corporation on Titan.


  • Overcome your enemies via tactical battleship combat, technological superiority, political influence, or the sheer productive power of your factories
  • Design the interiors of your battleships by strategically placing weapons, shields, engines, and more, reducing weak points and maximizing fighting capability
  • Explore the map to discover and exterminate hidden rebel encampments

GAME FEATURES:

  • Get to know your advisors with fully voiced lines and dialogue
  • Capture Titan's natural beauty with an in-game photo mode
  • Climb the leaderboards against real players in a Timed Contract mode
  • Gameplay is "real-time with pause" -- play at your preferred pace!
  • Created by the team that brought you Crypt of the NecroDancer, with art by Sir Carma and Nick Gunn, music by Danny Baranowsky, and audio by Power Up Audio
