What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early access is important for us because it allows us to collect the opinion and all the suggestions of the players in order to make the multiplayer aspect of the game correspond to its community and to the players. It allows us to improve existing features and add new ones based on player recommendations.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan to take the game out of the early access phase when we and the community are fully satisfied with the product. We estimate this to take about six months after the game's launch.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to add a lot of features that the community has requested, including new game modes, maps, weapons and gameplay mechanics.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of the early access version is detailed in the "About this game" section.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We do not plan any price changes.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The involvement of the community in the development process is paramount. Thanks to the many social networks on which we communicate such as Discord or Twitter, the community can propose and actively communicate with us at any time.”

NOTES BEFORE PURCHASING THE GAME

PROPHUNT™ is a game still in Early Access that was just recently released on Steam. Because of this, you may encounter some difficulties in finding games. We highly recommend you to buy the game with your friends to enjoy a perfect gaming experience. You can also join our community on Discord to find people to play with.

About This Game

Hide among the various props of the world, or find the imposters who are hiding among the props. Play as a hunter and flush out the players hidden in props or play as a prop who must hide from the hunters by morphing into objects.

Hunter

  • an assault rifle and pistol to eliminate the props
  • a grenade launcher that throws repulsive grenades to dislodge the props
  • a flashlight for better visibility in dark areas

Prop

  • a dynamic rotation system to easily hide
  • taunts to tease the hunters
  • almost all objects in the maps are imitable to hide

Features

  • realistic graphics
  • 2 game modes: simple and swap
  • 4 maps with dynamic game elements: Warehouse, Bunker, Hangar and Police station
  • host online games for up to 16 players
  • secure your games with passwords or invite your friends directly via Steam
  • join worldwide games via the server browser or quick game search
