Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“In order to get important feedback from the player, use the pre-experience mode.
The use of pre-experience mode was considered during The development period, because we felt it was important to communicate with players in a timely manner for The progress of The game. During this period, The team could discuss The feedback from everyone and turn The beasts of 9500 into a game with richer gameplay.
So, rufa, if you have any better Suggestions for the game, we'd love to discuss them with the players.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The idea is to expand the game frame indefinitely, add content, and optimize the game's art experience, so the pre-experience state will last for a year or so, but depending on how satisfied the player is with the game, we'll continue to add your comments to the game.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Over the next few months, we plan to add many new features, some of which are already in development, and we will continue to add new dinosaur species, including water and sky.Secondly, AI is a very important direction, we hope to create a very real dinosaur world, which includes all kinds of natural climate and small creatures.
With the improvement of the game, the pre-experience version will definitely change a lot. As the players become more familiar with the game, the old players may become less interested in the game, so we will continue to add new game modes.At least 3 different styles of terrain are planned for the future, including: forest, tropical rainforest and island.These terrainswill have a larger area, because our servers can support 300 people playing against each other at the same time, so as players grow, we will add larger maps to the game.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Although this is an Early Access release, the game already has a great gameplay:
The simulation of dinosaur survival has been fully realized.Players can play a variety of dinosaur characters, they must meet the basic needs of eating and drinking, then you can choose your evolution direction according to the growth points, maybe you are the same kind of dinosaur, but your final growth attributes will be different, it is the player's own decision.
- provides a dedicated game server, and has the service hosting function, the server owner will have special privileges, including game play, species restrictions, AI Settings, a variety of console commands and observation modes, player data will be permanently stored in the server.
- all necessary features such as options, Settings and graphics, sound adjustment
- chat function, team chat, world chat
-16 + dinosaur simulation experience
- group or single combat
- each dinosaur has more than 15 animations
-8k * 8k game scenes allow the player to explore”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“As the content expands and the quality improves, the price may increase moderately at launch, but for the first time players, you'll enjoy the game all the timeAs the content expands and the quality improves, you'll enjoy the game all the time”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“You can share your ideas and opinions in the community.We will actively listen to the feedback provided by the community and learn from the community's Suggestions on The beasts if 9500 to adjust our game plan.If we can get your support, we will open up interactive content in the community.”

About This Game

Discover the beast within and choose from the dozen of unique creatures to play as from predators like Tyrannosaurus rex and Sarchosuchus to herbivores like Pararsaurluphus. Explore beautiful landscapes and wildlife you may come across. Each Creature has their strengths and weaknesses but with the skill tree you can expand it further on your path to growth.The wildlife(AI) you discover will keep you on your toes because it is always a risk and reward to explore. You may conquer all the land by working together with other players. Players are free to challenge each other for sport or land/resources.

If you have any questions, please contact us on Discord：https://discord.gg/uvANcM3
