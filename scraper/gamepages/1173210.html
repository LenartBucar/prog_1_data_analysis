About This Content

What are Supporter Skins?

This is a collection of bonus skins branding the game's Logo in a stylish red glow. This is for those who wish to go the extra mile to support the developers of Horror Legends as the game evolves, more Supporter Skins will be added during the game's Early Access period.

Which characters currently come with a Supporter Skin?


Cast
  • Nick Scab
  • Angela Crass
  • Stevie Lint

Legends
  • Punkie
  • Killer Keith
  • Psycho Phil (added 1/22/2020)
  • Corey Sisley (added 1/22/2020)
  • Werewolf (added 6/25/2020)
  • Human-Punkie (added 8/01/2020)

When will I be getting more Supporter Skins?

Each major update will come with a hand full of new supporter skins for both Cast and Legend characters.


Thank you for all so much for your support. It means the world to us and we hope to continue to provide Horror Legends with new and exciting content for years to come.

-Drake.

Mature Content Description

The developers describe the content like this:

A series of skins that depict cartoonish blood splatter on each character.
