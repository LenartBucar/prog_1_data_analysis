Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We want to actively involve players in our development process. Players can submit their ideas for game content, and if enough people like it, it will be added to the game. This is only possible with early access. This will be a a 'community developed' game of sorts.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Between 6-12 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Similar gameplay loops, but with a much larger scope. Many more activities, dialogs, characters, and interactions will be added over the course of development.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“All features currently listed in the "about this" section are available, along with some extras (night club environment, exploration related 'hidden' secrets, etc). New content will be continually added throughout early access.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to gradually raise the price as we ship new content and features, with a final price goal of 3-5x the initial early access cost.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Participation of the community is very important, and a big reason why we decided on early access. All suggestions are welcome, and will be added to the game if we like them.”

About This Game

CUCKOLD SIMULATOR, the exciting new game where you play as Cuckold (that is his name) in his day-to-day life.

Features


  • Work your job at GloboCorp to make money
  • You can give all your hard-earned money to the bull
  • Browse your favorite websites, such as Reddit™
  • Your fat wife will verbally abuse you
  • Become emotionally attached to children that are not biologically yours
  • Collect 'Funky Pop' collectibles that are hidden across the world
  • Play actual games on your 'Wintendo Snitch' gaming console, such as 'Cuck Souls'
  • And more!

One Happy Family


  • Cuckold - Husband and beta male provider of the family. Only good for providing income.
  • Wife - Fat and mean. Only has eyes for Tyrone - and Cuckold's wallet
  • Tyrone - Transient alpha male of the household. Has outstanding warrants.
  • DeShawn - Wife's ten year old child from a previous relationship. Does not respect Cuckold.
  • Tyrone Jr. - The newest addition to the family. Named after his father. Too young to not respect Cuckold.

Wintendo Snitch


The Wintendo Snitch is a fully functional game console that you can purchase inside of the game. Play exciting games such as CUCK SOULS or Anime Girl Genocide. Truly a treat for fans of the soulsborne genre. More games to be released eventually.
