Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We’ve decided to release the game for Early Access to collect as much feedback as possible from the players, in order to improve the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The Early Access will last for around 1 year.
We will be fine-tuning and polishing the game according to feedback collected during EA.

Stage 1
- Fix all the major bugs in the game.（ Done）
- Add more adventures.（ Done）
- New Realms: Soul Formation and.（ Done）

Stage 2
- New Feature: Forge & Artifact. (Done）
- New function: Change Rewrite Destiny.（ Done）
- New function: Change Profile Image.（ Done）
- Game optimizations.（ Done）

Stage 3
- Optimization of Forge & Artifact.
- Feature redesign: Sect. Players will be able to attack other sects and become the Master.
- Mysterious organization.
- Super NPC.

Stage 4
- New feature: Incantation.
- Optimization: Feng Shui.
- New advanced skills.
- New Realms: Reborn and Transcendent.
- Release the main story: Xingtian Slayer.

Stage 5
- New Start Game Artifact: Eye of Providence.
- Optimization: Alchemy.

Stage 6
- Players' Workshop Ver. 1.0（Playes will be able to design adventures, stories, events, characters, monsters, and dungeons）

Stage 7
- New Start Game Artifact: Mythical Gourd. It'll allow players to collect monster souls and upgrade them.

Stage 8
- Unlock Tian Yuan Mountain.
- Players' Workshop Ver. 2.0: Players will be able to participate in a more complicated game design and improvements”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version of the game will come with all the chapters, providing the game's full story, with richer gameplay, new skills, characters, complete maps, and more main & side quests.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“There are 8 complete chapters in the Early Access version of the game, providing over 10 hours(average of 20-30 hours) of gameplay.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price of the game may increase after the release of the full version. But you can definitely expect the quality of the added features and the gameplay improvements.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“All lines of communication with the gaming community will be open. The main reason we are releasing the game for EA is to gather feedback from players, so we will actively respond to any comments we receive. Please feel free to reach us via social media such as Twitter, Discord, or through an e-mail to info@lightning.games
We’d love to hear from you!”

About This Game

Tale of Immortal is an open-world sandbox game based on Chinese mythology and cultivation. You will grow to become immortal, conquer the beasts from the Classic of Mountains and Season, make your choices carefully and grasp your own destiny.

Live the mythology

With a background based around ancient Chinese myths and stories, players will have a variety of interactive options with scenes and in-game NPCs, which will affect the storyline of the game and transform the game world.

Become Immortal

The theme of the game combines the immortal cultivation and the unique cultural background of The Classics of Mountain and Sea.
Players will experience the journey from a mortal to a strong, god-like being.

A word from the dev team:
Our studio hopes that in this game, players will express the theme of "Always stick to your heart, dare to fight against difficulties; make choices carefully and grasp your own destiny."
