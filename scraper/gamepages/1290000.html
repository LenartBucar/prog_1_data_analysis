Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Simulator games can fall anywhere on a spectrum from serious to daft. We think we know where the right point on that spectrum PWS should be, but we want to test our assumptions by launching into Early Access to get direct feedback from the buying public. Early Access will therefore be used to grow and work with a community that wants to support our game's development. We’ll be engaging with players to gather feedback and ideas that will shape the game and prioritise new, community-driven features!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Approximately 12 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will be a well-polished, well-rounded game that appeals to as broad an audience as possible. We plan to have multiple game modes, community implemented features and licensed content. Major updates during Early Access will include at least two multiplayer modes and regular new content drops. We have the potential to provide some unusual challenges with more experimental gameplay mechanics; the popularity of such challenges could inform the direction of the game's tone during production. ”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access release has had over a year’s development already, many of the core features are in and polished to a good standard. The game will have two modes; Career and Free Play, with various washers, nozzles, and cleaning fluids to use across over ten jobs ranging from small vehicles to larger architectural spaces, as well as a light and humorous story. We expect around 5 hours of gameplay for the Early Access release.

Whilst there may be some issues, we will be working to resolve these as soon as possible! Community members can contact us with feedback in game using the F8 key, across our social channels or the PowerWash Simulator Discord.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“It is likely the price will increase the more that content is added, but we have no conclusive plans at the moment.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The community's involvement is essential to us and to the development of the game. We have implemented a community feedback pipeline into the game which players can trigger by pressing F8 and filling in a short form. We encourage all feedback, ideas, and opinions, and we will gather these from our Steam Community, Discord and across our social channels daily. We will also be crediting new ideas brought explicitly by community members in Steam News posts and dev logs.”

About This Game



In the latest update, you can fuel a fairground rivalry by hosing the Helter Skelter to look as good as new and soak the shady Mayor's Frolic Boat to aid his hasty retreat. On top of that, the mysterious customer is back with a suspiciously souped-up up Private Jet, and a germophobic entrepreneur wants you to take on a putrid Public Washroom. It's a dirty job, but someone's got to do it!




Wash away your worries with the soothing sounds of high-pressure water. Fire up your power washer and blast away every speck of dirt and grime you can find. Build your own power-washing business and unlock new tools, upgrades and more – all with the simple satisfaction of power-washing to a sparkling finish. 

TIME TO CLEAN UP THE NEIGHBOURHOOD


Build up your business in Career Mode and complete a variety of dirty jobs across the dusty town of Muckingham.  There's no right or wrong; no time pressure or final score, just you and the tools you need to soak away your stress. Want a clean start? Chill out and replay your favourite jobs in Free Play. 

KEEP IT CLEAN


For those looking for a little more pressure, beat your best scores in Challenge Mode! Fight grime in different scenarios; wash against the clock in Time Challenge or test your accuracy by using as little water as possible in Water Challenge.

GOOD CLEAN FUN 


Graffiti, grime, moss, and mould, no dirt is too tough for your range of washers, nozzles, cleaners, and extensions. Different dirt types have different levels of toughness, so make sure you're getting tactical with your equipment. Feeling fancy? Then customise your look with washer skins and gloves!

RELAXATION, SATISFACTION GUARANTEED

From casual, clean freaks to players looking to get into the nitty-gritty, everyone can pick up and play to feel immersed. Absorb the relaxing atmosphere and stress-free pace as you strip dirt from patios, pavements, vehicles, and public parks. Stress gets to us all, so sit back, relax, and wash your worries down the drain.

THE WORLD IS YOUR CANVAS 

Create art by cleaning the way you want. Your nozzles are your brushes; the neighbourhood is your canvas. Unleash your creative talents and transform ordinary dirt into exceptional artwork. Share your techniques and showcase your power-washing prowess and mud-caked masterpieces!
