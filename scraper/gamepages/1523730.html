About This Content

Endless Space 2 - Dark Matter views the Endless Universe from the underbelly of events and discoveries that have, on occasion, been dark indeed…



From a series of quests investigating the strange events that preceded the fall of the Endless to simple problems of unruly subjects, enjoy the gamut of experiences and adventures from 12 new quests to discover, all set in the vast strangeness of the Endless Universe!
