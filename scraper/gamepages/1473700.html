About This Content

"RPG Maker MZ" official DLC released!

"RPG Maker MZ Odyssey of Fantasy Series" is a collection of materials that can express the world of high fantasy.
The battle background material collection focuses on the game fields such as forests in various parts of the high fantasy stages, Including more than 30 backgrounds for high fantasy game games such as towers and dungeons.

-Multiple graphic and texture materials for expressing different time zones of the day
Materials for forests and other sceneries include the morning, noon, and night materials.
Added multiple points such as light sources and moonlight instead of just changing the color to increase the world view's quality.

-Expand the world view by combining with the default materials
The user can use the battle background graphic materials and tile materials created for the RPG Maker MZ graphic standards seamlessly and perfectly with existing materials.

-Various expression by changing the combination of graphic parts
The user can change the combination of the upper half and the lower half graphics parts for towers and dungeons to expand a wide range of expressions.

-Tileset included
Includes auto tiles and wood tiles that match the battle background materials. You can recreate the world of high fantasy not only in battle scenes but also on maps.
