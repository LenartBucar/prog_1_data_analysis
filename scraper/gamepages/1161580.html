Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We’ve been working on Hardspace: Shipbreaker since early 2016, but we know we’re just scratching the surface in terms of the potential and promise this new genre and style of game holds. While we’re getting closer to being done in terms of features, gameplay ideas, storytelling, and much more – we want to give the game the time it needs, utilizing the awesome feedback we have received from our community.

On that note, we know there are an almost infinite number of options in terms of where we take this game, and which we do first. We want your help to determine that – what makes you most excited? What most needs fixing? We’ll regularly be asking you these questions to drive development in the correct direction to make Hardspace: Shipbreaker the absolute best game it can be.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We initially thought we would be in Early Access for one year. However, our great community of players have proven extremely valuable in identifying areas for further development, and we want to deliver an experience that feels complete. So, we've decided to continue in Early Access for a bit longer. We're not ready to share a 1.0 release date right now – but we can tell you we're working on finishing the game and getting it ready for full launch. You can have a look at our Living Roadmap to check the currently planned features before release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Here’s a brief rundown of our basic plans to expand on the above:
- A full campaign; master the art of Shipbreaking and make your way to the stars. We’re aiming for around 30 hours of gameplay
- A fourth ship class
- 3D HAB space to visit in between shifts
- Accessibility Options
- Steam achievements
- Loads of balancing, bug fixing, quality of life tweaks, and anything else we – and you – can come up with”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current Early Access version of Hardspace: Shipbreaker includes
- Act 1 of the campaign, featuring 5+ hours of gameplay
- Additional freeform sandbox mode and the R.A.C.E mode
- Multiple powerful and upgradeable salvage tools to help you tear apart spaceships and extract valuable components
- Three classes of spaceship to break apart
- Over a billion credits in debt to pay back
- Leaderboards and challenges to compare yourself against other players
- A variety of dangerous hazards and elements that make each ship a potential death trap”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“As we add content to the game we will re-evaluate our pricing – the timing and scale of such will depend on the rate of large updates and how much value we believe they bring to players.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“In every way possible! Between ourselves at Blackbird Interactive and our publisher Focus Home Interactive, we’re well equipped to take feedback on Discord, forums, social media, and everywhere else you might want to contact us. We’ll also be conducting live AMAs, Dev Vlogs, and other community initiatives as we head towards launch.

We hope players will enjoy the game as much as we enjoy making it!”

About This Game



Welcome to LYNX, the galaxy’s leading ship-salvaging corporation!



Across your career, you’ll have the privilege of paying your debt to us by purchasing salvaging rights to increasingly large and valuable ships. Cut them open and extract as much value as possible!



You are equipped with the latest in LYNX tech. Carve entry points, salvage valuable materials and components with your cutting tool or slice scrap metal at any angle into a million pieces as you ponder a lonely existence… the possibilities are endless! Be cautious – dying is extremely unprofitable.



We strongly advise upgrading your tools, helmet and suit to take on more lucrative contracts - all you need to worry about is how you’ll pay for it!

As of today, your debt amounts to:
999,999,999 Credits
Good luck on the job, cutter!



  • Experience daily life as a blue-collar spaceship salvager
  • Cut and destroy at will within a next-generation physics simulation
  • Upgrade tools and unlock new perks to take on harder contracts

JOIN THE COMMUNITY
Hardspace: Shipbreaker is a game in early access. Don’t hesitate to join our official Discord server and share your feedback with the development team so that we can improve Hardspace: Shipbreaker together.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
    • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel i5-6600K / AMD Ryzen 3 1300X
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: 4 GB VRAM, GeForce GTX 770 / Radeon R9 380
    • Additional Notes: 30 FPS in 1920x1080. The game is an Early Access game, and minimum specifications may change during development.
    Recommended:
    • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
    • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: Intel i7-8700 / AMD Ryzen 5 2600
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: 6 GB VRAM, GeForce GTX 980 Ti / Radeon RX Vega 56
    • Additional Notes: 60 FPS in 1920x1080. The game is an Early Access game, and recommended specifications may change during development.
