Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“
  • We want to continue developing our game together with community, assisted by their feedback. Especially working on game balance, improving existing features and adding more QoL features.
  • We feel like the current level of our product is closer to a solid early access, while full release version has few missing elements left desired such as a good soundtrack and content diversity.
”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We expect the full release in March 2022, 6 months from the early access start.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“
  • More scenarios, covering more different seafaring stories
  • More sailor occupations inside the ship, expanding inside-ship gameplay
  • More enemy variety and island quests
  • Solid soundtrack
  • Better VFX and genuinely enhanced graphics (based on scheduled engine upgrade)
  • Current state of optimization is quite poor, we have areas where we could drastically improve
  • Adding more localizations
  • Adding support for Mac and Linux
  • There is a possibility of adding controller support
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“This is left for our community to decide ;)”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We expect a 5$ increase in price for full release version.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan a monthly dev diary, we have active Discord, following up on steam community posts and reddit.”

About This Game


  • In-depth ship control - Directly control your ship, including steering, sails, rigging and make sure you avoid shoals or capsize the ship.
  • Life on board of a ship - Manage your crew shifts, plan provisions, repair hull damages and make sure crew spirits are high.
  • Diverse world - Explore the procedurally generated world full of diverse features: islands, reefs, shipwrecks, shoals and stormy regions.
  • Inland expeditions - Gather resources, solve mysteries and interact with suspicious natives in a classic text quest narrative adventure.
  • Strategic naval battles - Target enemy's vital systems, oversee crew performance and give orders, strengthening your tactics.
  • Develop your ship and crew[WIP] - Research upgrades, level up and specialize your sailors.
  • No second chances! - When you die, there's no coming back. The constant threat of defeat adds importance and tension to every action.
