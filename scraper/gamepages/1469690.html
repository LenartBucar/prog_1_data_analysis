Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The goal of making this game is to have almost perfect drifting fun. And how to achieve this if you don't involve community in it? I've done my best to put every basic aspect of Drift game - Tuning, cool cars and race tracks and etc. But I would like to fulfill the expectations of this game, so I decided to cooperate with the community and work on the game together with them.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The Early access will last approximately 8-10 months. During this period i will add more content - Cars, Race tracks, Game modes, will fix bugs...Also i will carefully listen to community and will consider their best suggestions for the game. I plan to follow very strict plan and i'll try my best to launch update in every two weeks.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version of the game will offer way much different content...many more cars, tracks and game modes (including multiplayer), also more polished gameplay.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Currently game has:
  • 23 different cars
  • Regular drift circuit mode
  • Mountain Drift game mode
  • Endless drifting game mode
  • 27 unique drift tracks
  • Car tuning - engine upgrade, Dyno, paint, Disk/rims replacement, custom skin/decals creator
  • Online Multiplayer free roam - create a room with up to 4 players and GO!
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“the price will be lower for the entire period of early access. Once all the key elements have been introduced and the game has been fine-tuned, the price will be raised. (Don't worry, the price won't be doubled)”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Collecting feedback and suggestions from the community will be very important to me. Game will have numerous ways to contact with community, Steam forums, twitter or discord, Thanks to the feedback from the players, I will be able to refine the main features of the game to meet the expectations of Drift king players”

About This Game

LET's DRIFT!

Grab your favorite car, tune it and challenge other players online

Customize car With your own way - upgrade Engine, change rims, colours, create custom Liveries :

Conquer challenging Drift Tracks, climb up on leaderboard and become DriftKing
