Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The game is close to completion, but we would like to gather community feedback as we complete the game and help to build a much better experience. The feedback from players is insanely valuable to us.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“3-6 Months”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“For the full version of the game, we have planned a considerable amount of content:

  • Complete story
  • 3 new levels
  • Introduce new enemy and mechanic on each new level
  • More weapons
  • Improvements and player feedback incorporated into the game
  • Wave Survival Arena
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“It showcases the game mechanics and look and feel of the game.

What is included in the Early Access version:
  • The first 3 levels
  • Numerous range and melee weapons
  • Different type of enemies in each level
  • Puzzles and traps
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Yes, the game will increase in price when it is released.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We intend to listen to suggestions from the community and implement anything that we feel really improves the overall gameplay or graphics.
We will be listening to feedback on the Steam Community and our Discord group.
We're excited to hear what everyone has to say about the game.”

About This Game



Ravensword: Undaunted is a hexen-like shooter RPG spinoff set within the Ravensword universe, focusing on dungeon crawling experience with intense melee and staff combat.

TERRIFYING DISCOVERY

Ancient scriptures talk about a myth of the existence of The Lightlands as the opposite side of Shadowlands, both harmonizing in synergy manifested as reality. With Shadowlands being overrun and torn by Demons of the Underworld, no one knows what happens to Lightlands until it became forgotten and lost to time. Until one fateful day, the Lightlands seemingly revealed itself back to humanity. Rifts to the unknown started to appear on Tyreas, and its elusiveness baited many adventurers, and none return. Takes the role as one of the Kingâs Hidden, an organization that seeks and destroys any imminent threat to the land, tasked to investigate various Rifts to seek the truth on whatâs going on.

HACK AND SLASH UNTIL IT'S DONE

With varying degrees of an arsenal left by the cults and inhabitants of the mysterious island, use it to overcome the vile armies and earn shards, the only currency accepted by inhabitant of the island. Slash through hordes of undead with any Swords, Maces, and Axes you unlocked; or outsmart them with various devastating Staves that can obliterate them in a second.


A GATEWAY TO THE PAST, AND FUTURE

Traverse through the Lightlands and discover various rifts of realities within, a portal to alternate past, and a grim prophecy of the future. Travel into an altered future of Tyreas where humanity has ceased to exist. Beware and tread lightly, as the Lightlands has been overrun by a mysterious being called âThe Shapesâ, seemingly tinkering with time, making the Lightlands dangerous and the alternate dimension all corrupted.


TRAVEL THROUGH FORSAKEN PLACES

From foggy wood outskirts to damp abandoned old castle, explore an ever-shifting places infested by undead in a stage-based world system with various encounter types waiting for your death. Explore, plunder, and send the island back to the void.


AN ARSENAL OF TIME

Unlock various melee and magic weapons and improve their lethality by upgrading their intrinsic potency, if you have what it requires.
