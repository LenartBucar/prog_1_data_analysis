Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The game will be released in episodic format, just like its predecessor. This time it will just happen in Early Access, to prepare for a better and bigger launch once all the content is ready!

While I have the basic outline ready for future episodes, the details and focus of them will change upon player feedback. Early Access allows me to gather more player feedback from the start, to make sure the game supports a multitude of play styles!

And last but not least; I need the help. Over the years the Lakeview Cabin games have grown bigger, and thus take more time to develop. Buying the game while in EA helps me keep food on the table for my two kids.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I'm aiming to release it within 1,5-2 years. There will be new episodes to play during this period, with updates to the older ones posted frequently.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will include 4 big episodes, each a unique horror experience. The game also contains a hub level that connects everything together, and the final version will have a few additional surprises...”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The hub level is there, along with the first episode. 2-player split screen co-op is also implemented.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price will increase by a small amount once the game leaves Early Access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“While I have a general outline of what the upcoming episodes will be, the canvas is still blank.
Getting involved this early will help me shape the rest of the game into a more complete package, where hopefully every type of player will have a gruesome but fun time.

Those who've dived a bit deeper into the Lakeview-Universe probably already know how open I am with my development cycle. We already have a great Discord-server in place, where I'm always available to answer any questions and concerns.
I'll be checking out the Steam forums on a regular basis, and I'm also always available for contact on Twitter!

Discord: https://discord.gg/3Ww5nFa
Twitter: https://twitter.com/Hypnohustler”

About This Game

"This time, the terror is real..."

Play as four companions as they use anything they can find to survive against terrifying foes. Explore the possibilities of emergent gameplay and dive deeper into the lore of the Lakeview. Just make sure you have a plan before the night falls...

-Sequel to Lakeview Cabin Collection (2015)
-First episode is in the game, with three more to come!
-Play alone or with your friend! 2-player CO-OP available from the start!
