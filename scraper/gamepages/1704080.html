Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“The game already provides a complete experience, but lacks some final touches and can be expanded with further content updates. Our aim is to expand and reach the fullest potential of Bean Stalker with the help of our community’s feedback. We want our players to have an impact on the final game, by engaging them in conversation.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The plan is to leave Early Access within a year.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The current version of Bean Stalker contains 2 different procedurally generated worlds, each with its own unique set of tools and challenges.
To the full version, we plan to add more content, at least one new world, longer playtime, and a multiplayer mode.
Everything is already prepared for the following updates; the multiplayer framework is in place and the design is planned out for the new world. What we still have to do is test the designs in order to achieve the best VR experience imaginable.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Our first two worlds are completed and each has a unique set of enemies, tools, weapons, and challenges for players to encounter. Our third map is mostly done, however, it still requires thorough testing and balancing. We plan to provide up-to-date information on our feedback-based process every few weeks or months.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price during and after Early Access will differ. We would like to reward players who put their trust in Bean Stalker in an early state by letting them access the game at a lower price. As more content is introduced, we want to increase the price to reflect the difference and match the quality of the game.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Working with player feedback is one of the main reasons why we provide Early Access. Since we are working with a small team, community feedback will be needed to reach the fullest potential of our game.”

About This Game

The branches reach up to the sky, and the beanstalk provides a link between Earth and the Upper world. Every continent and every climate rises from the underworld. An endless horde of creatures descend from the sky and crawl out from the depths. You must hunt creatures and adapt to a new way of vertical life. Become a Stalker of this New Realm. Use your Blade Slammer and turn into more than a new hunter of this new world.
Evolve, create, develop to be the most skilled among all, and defeat the spirit of the tree on top.



  • Endless variations
    Every time you put on your headset, the realm of the beanstalk will be different. The game includes procedurally-generated random vertical worlds with immense verticality, sandbox-like elements and constantly changing quests and challenges. You will never solve a quest or find an adventure the same way twice, so do not forget to make the best shots of your gameplay.



  • You cannot climb the same way twice
    Use your Blade Slammer not only for face-to-face fights but to wander around the branches and reach the top. Experience the feeling of real climbing with the unique vertical platform elements, from the comfort of your own room.



  • Survive
    Unique challenges wait around every corner. Beware of the creatures of the beanstalk; they have already fought their way to the highest branches for the sweetest of saps. Defeat them to gain the most valuable resources.
    Don't hesitate to leave the tree and save your skin. You can always come back wielding better equipment and be ever ready to defeat the foes that block your path to the top where you will face the spirit of the tree



  • Craft
    You’ll need to craft different tools and solve quests to reach higher levels and face the spirit of the tree on top. You can craft swords, crossbows, shields, grenades, and grappling hooks to make your movement more efficient across the branches. Don't forget to use your Book of Crafts!
Brace yourself for a brand new adventure in this strange world!
