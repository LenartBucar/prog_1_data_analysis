Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Early access will give us the possibility to work on the game and get feedback during its development.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Some time in 2021.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The biggest improvement will be a completed story. As of right now the first out of three acts is finished.
The first act will also get improvements as a final touch to the full release.
Another addition will be more gameplay features to improve the overall experience.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“>The story is half way done while there are still plans for adding side quests and random stories and attractions to explore.

>The mini job system is a proof of concept and will be later expanded into full-fledged job mini games, where in you will be able to do various tasks by demand of costumers in the game.

As a general estimate the game is 40% complete.
(The content not relevant to the story will be introduced in a large content update)

A full listing of the features in-game can be found in the "About this" section.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“As of right now there is no plan on raising the price any higher.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The Community will be involved in suggesting what features they would like to have included (while evaluating if it will fit the general feeling of the game) or what they would like to have changed.

We have an active community of about 300 people over at Discord, where we can be contacted directly and give the latest updates and news on the game first hand!
https://discord.gg/EG2wkT7”

About This Game

After you escaped with a time machine from a secret facility you go on a trip through time.
Visit all possible time periods with specific jobs and quests for each of them. But watch out for the Time Agents. And avoid yourself!

This is THE Time Travel Simulator. A nice mix of simulator, story and action, "No Time" takes you on a trip through time. Just type any date from 0 AD onwards...
Above all make sure to avoid yourself and not to behave out of place in the past!

Main Features:

- An open world that changes based on the time period

- A Full-fledged Day and Night cycle with weather and seasons!

- Job minigames

- A main story with up to 10 hours of playtime!

- Side Quests!

- Your own time machine you can modify to your likings!

- A time travel system with features like
>going to any time from 0-2030 AD
>venturing through the story by going into various time periods
>being able to see your past actions in time!

- A combat system (use any items around you to defend yourself)

- A crafting system
