Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Steam Early Access is an excellent opportunity for us to begin the next stage of development for Tower Unite.

There are still a lot of features we plan to implement, and releasing on Early Access will allow us to exceed the expectations of our fans and our community.

We want to ensure our community is part of our development throughout the process. Our game is unique due to it's diverse, varying gameplay. We want to expand the game based on feedback in ways that we could never imagine on our own.

Early Access allows us to realize our dream game. We want to create the best possible experience for a wide audience, and we can't do that alone. We will use Early Access to address concerns, gather additional data from gameplay experiences, balance the game, and add new features to the game from community feedback.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We hope to leave Early Access sometime in 2021, but will take more time if absolutely needed.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to ship with more Game Worlds, all the various planned Plaza activities, additional player avatar customization, ocean exploration, more advanced condo customization tools, additional Workshop support, and the features we promised our backers. We also want to ship the game on Mac OS and Linux.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game currently features:
  • 6 Game Worlds:
    • Minigolf with 9 playable maps with 18 holes each (162 holes total)
    • Zombie Massacre with 4 playable maps
    • Little Crusaders with 6 playable maps
    • Ball Race with 9 playable maps
    • Virus with 5 playable maps
    • Accelerate (Kart Racing) with 4 playable maps & vehicle workshop support
  • Arcade with 22 playable games!
  • Nightclub with billiards and synchronized music!
  • Casino with 8 playable games:
    • Texas Hold'em
    • 3 different slot machines
      • Triple Diamonds: A basic 3 reel slot machine
      • Grand Quest: A 3 reel slot machine with RPG elements
      • Wheel of Money: A 3 reel slot machine with a bonus money wheel
    • Video Poker
    • Video Blackjack
    • Spin to Win
    • Double or Nothing
  • Plaza games and activities:
    • Fishing (fish over 100 different types of fish)
    • Laser Tag
    • Bowling
    • Casino
    • Trivia
    • Random Events/Minigames
    • Theater with YouTube™ and Soundcloud support
    • Typing Derby
    • The Poseidon roller coaster
    • Monorail
    • Seasonal Events such as Halloween and Winter Holidays
    • Various minigames such as Balloon Shoot and Target Practice
  • Create custom models and import them directly to the game with Steam Workshop
  • 1800+ unique unlockable items that are interactive and customizable
  • Rank up and earn badges and items during gameplay (over 70+ items and 120 badges to earn)
  • Over 349 achievements to unlock (achievements also give out EXP and Units)
  • Leaderboard support
  • Customize yourself in each game world (play Minigolf as a basketball, bowling ball, etc.)
  • The Condo (personal house) with customizable furniture, wallpaper, weather, day/night cycle editing, and several different locations
  • Host a Condo for friends to join easily
  • Build with your friends in real time!
  • Share pictures, video, and custom models from the internet to your friends in your Condo
  • Synchronized YouTube™ and Soundcloud media players
  • Playable instruments such as a piano and a drum-set
  • Full Steam inventory support
  • P2P voice chat
  • Player hosted servers
  • P2P Condo to Plaza communication
  • 22+ different in-game item shops
  • Game World matchmaking (traveling to and from Game World instances)
  • Basic character customization and accessories
  • Basic gamepad support (not inventory)
  • Extended parental controls to control what content you see
  • Workshop support for player models, items, and vehicles!
  • Download and enjoy various user world creations on the Condo Workshop

    The game is currently Windows only, but can be played reliably on Linux using Steam Play

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price may change during early access and possibly after early access, as we ship new content and features.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Since it's inception, Tower Unite has been all about the players.
By listening to our community, we have been able to build a game that has broad appeal
across multiple genres.
We are constantly welcoming new people into our diverse community to contribute ideas to the game.
If we find ideas that our players will enjoy, we start developing those concepts into new features for Tower Unite.

Our game has always depended on it's fans, they crowdfunded us to bring this to life.
In return, we will be constantly updating the game with new features, and bug fixes.

We also have live streams and weekly developer logs with production updates from the developers.

You can join in on our community today by heading to our forums: http://forums.pixeltailgames.com
We are very active on our forums and are there to answer any questions you may have.”

Tower Unite is a living, evolving world, fueled by the community. Play online games with your friends or make new friends from around the world!


Tower Unite is a large online multiplayer game that spans several genres.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
    • OS: Windows 7 SP1 (64-bit)
    • Processor: 2.4 GHz Dual-Core 64-bit CPU
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: DX10 Compatible GPU with 2 GB VRAM
    • DirectX: Version 10
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 15 GB available space
    • Additional Notes: Integrated graphic cards will have trouble running the game. Disk space will increase over time due to new content added.
    Recommended:
    • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
    • OS: Windows 7 SP1 (64-bit), Windows 8 (64-bit), Windows 10 (64-bit)
    • Processor: 3 GHz Quad-Core 64-bit CPU
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: DX11 Compatible GPU with 4 GB VRAM
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Network: Broadband Internet connection
    • Storage: 20 GB available space
