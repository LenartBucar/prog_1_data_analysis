What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“"Bloody Spell" is an action-based role-playing game developed by Elong Games. It has been developed by 3 people for more than 3 years. Although it has been difficult (the game industry in 2018 understands the big environment), we will not give up!
We are developers and players. We hope to make the game more perfect in this mode, so that everyone can actively participate and complete the creation of " Bloody Spell ".”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Hi,

I am Uncle Li, the producer of'BloodySpell'. Today is the Chinese New Year. I wish you all good health and the best in the new year! Then I will report to you the development situation and follow-up plans of the game since it went online.

‘BloodySpell’ is currently being developed smoothly. There are currently 3 team members, and the Early Access version has been trusted and supported by many players. We thank you again.

Up to now,'BloodySpell' has undergone 5 major updates and dozens of minor updates. Because of your suggestions and comments on each update of the game, the game will continue to improve.

Let us review the important updates since the launch of'BloodySpell':
2019-2-2 Increase blood palace mode challenge, etc.

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will add the following:
- 8 large maps
- new weapon type
- New equipment and props
- New mobs, elite monsters, BOSS
- New bloodthirsty skills
- New cutscenes and text plots
- New mini games
- new controllable roles
- New background music and plot dubbing
- Multiplayer mode
The above will increase as the map expands.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The early access version is a beauty action game. It currently has 9 main-line levels. It takes about 6 hours to get through the main-line normally. It also includes multiplayer online, massive skins, blood palace mode, kick hall mode, hell mode, creative workshop and other interesting gameplay. The total experience time can reach more than 18 hours. Since the current version of the action system is relatively complete, has more skills and equipment, levels have more random elements, and interspersed with puzzle-solving elements and small game play, there will be no repeated experience of brushing pictures.
It should be noted that most of the plot of this game is relatively obscure, and key information needs to be obtained from the npc or the scene. The current version of the plot information is less and requires careful exploration by the player.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Due to the increase in game content and increased cost in all aspects, we plan to increase prices with the release of the full version.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We will carefully read every feedback in the " Bloody Spell " community, actively discuss with the players how to make the game better, and follow-up development and adjustment of the game based on the feedback from the players.”

Solution to the black screen and crash problem when entering the game:
1. Download and install the K-Lite Codec Pack decoder, the decoder download address: https://pc.qq.com/detail/8/detail_528.html
2. Go to the root directory of BloodySpell and find BloodySpell.exe, right-click on properties, run compatibility mode, and then disable full-screen optimization.
3. Unplug the wireless controller receiver or turn off Bluetooth
4. Check the integrity of the game
5. Upgrade the graphics driver

When entering the game, there are problems such as the untouched start game button and UI display error:
Step 1: alt f4 to turn off the game.
Step 2: Delete the Savesdir folder in the root directory of Bloodthirsty Seal.
Step 3: Reopen the game and follow the prompts to download cloud archives.


"Bloody Spell" is a martial arts action role-playing game. The core of the game is battle-oriented, and it combines many fighting elements and systems.

Ten years ago, Yejin and his sister Li went to practice martial arts under the sect of Wanfa. Since then, they have been far away from the complicated human world.Ten years later, the mysterious organization that claimed to be Void Legion invaded the sect, and then the world was in chaos, and the brothers and sisters were reinvested in the chaotic world.The sisters who are dependent on each other are taken hostage, and the disciples have been killed in one by one... At the moment when the bloodthirsty spell is opened, Yejin who knows himself, can he fulfill his commitment to everyone? And who is the last to be able to help the building, to save the devastated world?

In terms of combat, this game uses multi-weapon switching as the main feature, and for each weapon in different speeds, power and positioning, design a variety of different skills, a variety of skills combo, greatly enhance the game cool feeling, excellent hitting and handling feel make it even better.

The highlight of the level design is the form of random combination, that is, the scenes, monsters, equipment, and configuration of the props that the player is reborn after death are different from before, so that the game presents diverse characteristics, increasing the level variables and The game is fun.

The "Bloody Spell" system is that, Yejin uses special skills in the body's evil spirit curse to achieve various effects such as violent walking, bullet time, roar breaking, assassination execution, counterattack and blood return, etc., which is to enhance the character's attack power and auxiliary combat. And to simplify the battle, add to the character and weapon attributes, and some even play a role in determining the battle.

The system makes the combat part more flexible, more selective and versatile, while also ensuring the smoothness and refreshment of the battle. In addition, many interesting side quests, diverse survey elements, and mini games interspersed with them greatly expand the game's fullness and playability.

We hope to see ten different players in ten different ways. If the enemy's threat is too great, you can use the bloodthirsty skills, assassinations, flying props, scene agencies to destroy them. Finally, please be careful in this Survive in the game.
