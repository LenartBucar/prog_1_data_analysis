About This Content

Rivellon will come to life wherever you are, with the original soundtrack of metacritic must-play RPG Divinity: Original Sin 2. Composed by Borislav Slavov, the Composer and Music Director of Larian Studios, this full digital album includes 42 tracks, equalling over two hours of music for your listening pleasure.

Note: This is the same soundtrack as included in the Divine Ascension DLC.
