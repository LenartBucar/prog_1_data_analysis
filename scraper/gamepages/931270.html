Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“I believe that even a small city-builder is a complex enough game to benefit greatly from early feedback and iteration. I would like to involve interested players to hear which ideas work best and where to direct the focus of the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The original plan was for 2 to 6 months. However, great feedback means I can expand the game and add much more content than originally planned. The current plan is around 1 year or longer.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“
  • Scenarios and campaign-like mode with goals
  • More and expanded production chains
  • More in-depth villager needs and interactions
  • Governing policies and perks
  • Trading
  • Improvements to graphics
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“
  • Random terrain generation
  • 70+ unique buildings and auxiliary work stations
  • 50+ items from resources and materials to food and supplies
  • Villager AI – delivering items, working in buildings
  • Villager housing, food, and happiness mechanics
  • Technology tree and knowledge gathering
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price will be raised moderately before release to reflect the added content.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I would include players in deciding which potential gameplay features to focus on: from low-level production chain ideas and improvements, to high-level potential game goals, modes and governing.”

About This Game



Build and manage your growing town in this relaxing village construction and management simulator with focus on production chains and villager desires.



Start with nothing and grow into a bustling town. Gather and process resources and maintain your villager needs



Collect and record knowledge, research and advance through technologies, and unlock new materials and professions.



Establish many production chains with unique processing buildings and outputs.



Everything from mining ores and crafting tools to growing crops and brewing beer.



Mr. Piggles agrees.
