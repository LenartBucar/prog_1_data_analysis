Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“At the moment this game is completed, it is difficult to adjust game balance. But it is easy to do in development while receiving feedback from users.
Because this game is an independent production, there is a trend to be smug. But by receiving feedback from user, the problem will be solved.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Between 2 and 3 years I think.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Alpha2.1.0

Alpha2.2.0 or later
- Add minimap
- Add random map
- Moving the map by transports
- Key configuration
- Remake of attack motion
- Relationship between game stage and enemy strength
- Add NPCs (both enemies and allies)
- NPC route search
- Add followers
- Add quests
- Add armor items that changes appearance

ON HOLD
- Multiplay
- Underground world
- Story mode”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“For example, you can do the following.
- Pick a stone to craft a stone knife.
- Cut trees with a stone knife.
- Craft a stone pickaxe from stones and wood.
- Mine iron rocks to get iron ore and craft iron ingots by a forge.
- Craft iron axes of iron ingots.
- Beat zombies by iron axe.
- Use iron bucket to get seawater then craft a clean water from seawater. You can craft salt from seawater too.
- Plow the field and sow seeds. After you water the plants, you can get crops.
- Dig the ground to set wood spikes to protect from zombies.
- Play the billiards with friendly NPCs.
- Attack the NPC to loot their items.
- You can fish.
- Eclipse Horde comes every 30 days.
- Weather such as foggy and thunderstorm. Temperature and humidity affects your status.
- Tame sheeps and cows and collect wool and milk from them.
- Tamed animals poop.
- Build sailboats and airships and escape from the world (2D side-scrolling shooter)
- You can improve your skills such as science and blacksmithing to do more crafting.
- You can burn combustibles with Molotov cocktails and flamethrowers.
- You can capture zombies to generate electricity and install electric traps.

About This Game

"Craft or die" It's up to you to decide.

Your streaming is always welcome!

Let's survive in zombie world! You can cut trees and mine ores, plow the field, craft items.
For example, you can do the following.
* Pick a stone to craft a stone knife.
* Cut trees with a stone knife.
* Craft a stone pickaxe from stones and wood.
* Mine iron rocks to get iron ore and craft iron ingots by a forge.
* Craft iron axes of iron ingots.
* Beat zombies by iron axe.
* Use iron bucket to get seawater then craft a clean water from seawater. You can craft salt from seawater too.
* Plow the field and sow seeds. After you water the plants, you can get crops.
* Dig the ground to set wood spikes to protect from zombies.
* Play the billiards with friendly NPCs.
* Attack the NPC to loot their items.
* Lets fishing!
