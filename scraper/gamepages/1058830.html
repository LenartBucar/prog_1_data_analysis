Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Spin Rhythm will enter early access to receive player feedback and build a community around our player driven game features including additional difficulty levels, difficulty optimisation, track list expansion, a polished level editor, as broad as possible midi controller (DJ gear) support, improved backgrounds and more.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“The game will be in early access for around 12-18 months, with support planned for 1-2 years after full release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“CONTENT & DIFFICULTY

Early access launch will include 15 levels, with a further 15 minimum levels to be added throughout development, bringing the total to over 30. We will ship with three difficulties, Normal, Hard and Expert, and we will add Easy (simplest) and XD (most challenging) as development continues.

LEVEL EDITOR

Much of development will involve creating a robust, fully-featured level design interface.

Along with polishing the backgrounds already in the game, we hope to allow users to hook up their custom level designs to colourful background events that synchronise with the music, dynamic twists and turns in the track flight path and more.

MIDI CONTROLLER SUPPORT

We would love to be able to support as many midi devices and DJ controllers as possible for when KBAM just isn't fulfilling your interdimensional DJ dreams. Currently we have simple tools allowing for detection and calibration of many different midi devices, but these will need to be polished and tested across a wide variety of devices with the help of our community. Please don't go buying $2,000 DJ decks before we have this finalised.

A note: the DJ Hero controller doesn't work well with the game. The wheel itself doesn't pick up taps, and it's quite heavy and loose, not designed for multiple spins..

ORIGINAL SOUNDTRACK (OST)

Some of our OST tracks (made specifically for the game) are still being written. we hope to finalise these during early access, and release an official Spin Rhythm originals soundtrack from artists such as Hyper Potions, Maxo, Birocratic, 2Mello, Kitty, Douglas Holmquist, seejay, Daverwob and more. Our community will be able to directly support the artists they love through purchase of the OST.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Spin Rhythm is currently in a fully playable beta version with over 40 playable tracks across 5 difficulty levels. We also have a tutorial, basic midi calibration tools and KB&M / controller calibration. Other features include daily challenges and SpinFM (online multiplayer).

Currently we feature around 10 unique backgrounds between two background styles (sand crystals and wireframe.

As development progresses, we hope to add a unique background for each level that reacts to the music.

Refer to above section for full details.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Upon leaving Early Access to 1.0 (and Switch launch) the game will increase slightly in price.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Get on board at our Twitter https://twitter.com/SpinRhythmGame, our Discord server at https://discord.gg/spinrhythmxd. You can also hit up Steam discussions with any technical issues.

All levels are hand sequenced to the music with difficulty built using player analytics and feedback. The more ambitions components of our game are centred around player-driven community and contribution.

Player feedback will be important in developing robust level editing tools. We hope these tools will be capable of supporting any song a player could think of no matter how complex the song structure.

We lack the ability to test every midi device on the market, but our tools are currently able to detect a huge range of midi chatter and map this chatter to gameplay functions. We hope our community can help us get our MIDI calibration tools to recognise and support as many devices as possible.”

About This Game

Spin Rhythm XD is an electronic music rhythm game with fluid, analogue-inspired controls and fully hand-made levels.

FEATURES

  • TAKE CONTROL. Spin, flick and tap to match colours and beats. Spin Rhythm's smooth controls work seamlessly across multiple devices including mouse and keyboard, gamepads and even real MIDI DJ turntables (touch-sensitive jogs recommended).
  • MASTER. Precise, hand-made levels across 5 difficulties (Easy, Normal, Hard, Expert and the brutal XD difficulty)
  • MUSIC. Over 40 licensed tracks of the juiciest beats in the known universe from Nitro Fun, Hyper Potions, Camellia, Subtact, Panda Eyes, Teminite, Anomalie, Tut Tut Child, Pegboard Nerds, Tristam, Braken, Maxo, Tokyo Machine, Douglas Holmquist, Kitty, Rogue, seejay, 2Mello and more.
  • CREATE. Custom levels to your favourite tunes using our fully-featured level editor.
  • ENJOY. Comprehensive accessibility features including custom colours, track speeds, low impact visuals and more.
  • COMPETE. Global leaderboards, daily challenge, weekly challenges with mutators (coming soon!) and more.
