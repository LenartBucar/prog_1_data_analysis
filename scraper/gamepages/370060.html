Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“This is the first big game I'm releasing and I have developed it by myself, that is why community feedback is so important to me, I want to build a game that you will enjoy and love, and the best way to achieve that is to hear you loud and clear, by publishing Coffee Shop Tycoon as early access I have more freedom to tweak and craft the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“I have a set of objectives that I want to achieve before considering it a full release, my top priority is to create enough content for you. From the beginning I always envisioned the game with many different coffee shops and styles, from furniture to customers, allowing you to customize your store however you prefer.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full versions will have a wider variety of styles and coffee shops. Game mechanics will be more polished and balanced and overall you will have more things to discover.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of the game features two coffee shops where you can hire employees, brew coffee, and customize your store. It includes almost every feature planned for the game. During early access I will keep adding more coffee shops and items.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The game will have a higher price after Early Access since you will be getting more content. However by supporting the game during Early Access you get to pay less and play if before anyone else.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“I will be actively interacting with the community and opening discussions to suggest changes and new features.”

About This Game

Coffee Shop Tycoon is a management game in which you take control of your own coffee shop. Will you thrive and become a popular coffee shop or die trying? Everything lies in your hands.

The game is NOT another 'click and wait three hours for your employees to cook' kind of game you will have to take choices constantly that will bring more costumers or make you lose money. We are building a game in which customization and planning is as important as having fun.

Brew It!

What's the first thing you want in the morning? Yes, a hot coffee. You will be able to make your customers love you or hate you. Get your beans, grind them with love and brew them with passion, and hopefully, you will touch someone's heart with your barista skills.

Have a crazy coffee idea? Go wild! More sugar? of course, whipped cream? Why not? Bacon? Well... not that wild. The options are endless. Create your own seasonal beverages, and build a reputation for your shop.

Let It Bloom

Sharpen your management skills and build a coffee empire! Hire and train the best in the biz while keeping your company wealthy and resourceful. Need some extra cash? Get a loan! Need more customers? Spread the word through marketing campaigns! Become the ultimate tycoon and conquer the world from your company's headquarters.

Your store your style

Delight your customers with your unique style, buy new furniture and decorations to create a vibrant atmosphere. Industrial, modern, maybe classic? It is up to you, paint the walls and change the floors. Immerse yourself in your own store.

Explore new locations and find new challenges. Become the best coffee shop in the world.

Stonks?

A new coffee shop just opened, are you going to let them take clients from you? Be a shark. Compete against AI stores and buy them out, become the ultimate legal monopoly on the world and unlock unique perks and logos.

Features

  • Brew your coffee
  • Create your own drinks
  • Hire and train staff
  • Unlock specializations for your employees
  • Marketing campaigns
  • Seasons and trends
  • Buy and sell stocks from other companies
  • Unique locations with different styles
  • Build reputation with specialty drinks and exquisite food
  • Choose and customize your coffee shop logo
  • Buy furniture and decoration for your coffee shop
  • Research and add services to your store (Wifi, rewards, and more)
  • Paint your walls and customize your floors
  • Special events
  • Manage key resources
  • Manage your franchise from you headquarters
